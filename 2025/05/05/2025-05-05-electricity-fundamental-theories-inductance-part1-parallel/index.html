High School Basic Electricity: Inductance and Electromagnetism - Inductance Part I Example: Parallel Inductance Calculation - 高中基本電學：電感與電磁_電感量_PART I 範例：並聯總電感的運算

Posted on 2025-05-05 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article explains how to calculate the total inductance of parallel inductors, both with and without considering mutual inductance. It also discusses the effect of the dot convention on mutual inductance in parallel circuits.

引言

本文說明如何計算並聯電感的總電感量，包含不考慮與考慮互感的情況，並介紹互感方向（打點法則）對並聯電感計算的影響。

Key Points

  1. Parallel Inductance Calculation (Ignoring Mutual Inductance):
    $$L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}$$
    Example: For L1=20mH and L2=30mH, total inductance is 12mH.
  2. Parallel Inductance Calculation (Considering Mutual Inductance):
    $$L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}$$
    Example: For L1=8H, L2=2H, and M=2H, total inductance is 2H.
  3. Mutual Inductance Direction (Dot Convention):
    The direction of mutual inductance (dot convention) affects the calculation. Head-to-head and tail-to-tail with dots in the same direction is mutual assistance in parallel.

重點歸納

  1. 並聯電感計算（不考慮互感）：
    $$L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}$$
    例：L1=20mH，L2=30mH，總電感為12mH。
  2. 並聯電感計算（考慮互感）：
    $$L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}$$
    例：L1=8H，L2=2H，M=2H，總電感為2H。
  3. 互感方向（打點法則）：
    互感方向（打點法則）會影響計算，頭接頭尾接尾且打點同向屬於互助並聯。
