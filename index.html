Basic Electricity 1 - Section 3-7: Kirchhoff’s Current Law

Key Points

  • Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL): At any node in an electrical circuit, the total current entering equals the total current leaving.
  • Formula:
    $$
    \sum I_{\text{in}} = \sum I_{\text{out}}
    $$
    or algebraic sum equals zero:
    $$
    \sum I = 0
    $$
  • The algebraic sum of currents at a node (inflows positive, outflows negative) is zero.
  • Use examples to calculate currents and find unknown values.
  • In parallel circuits, node currents conserve and voltages are equal.
  • Examples include calculating currents and resistances to verify KCL.

磨課師基本電學1 3-7 克希荷夫電流定律

重點歸納

  • 克希荷夫電流定律 (KCL)：在電路中任一節點，流入的總電流等於流出的總電流。
  • 公式表示：
    $$
    \sum I_{\text{流入}} = \sum I_{\text{流出}}
    $$
    或代數和為零：
    $$
    \sum I = 0
    $$
  • 節點電流的代數和（流入為正，流出為負）等於零。
  • 透過實例計算節點電流，求出未知電流值。
  • 並聯電路中，節點電流守恆，電壓相等。
  • 實例中計算電流和電阻，驗證KCL定律。

2-4.1 Ohm’s Law Practice Examples

Summary

  • Ohm’s Law formula:
    $$ R = \frac{V}{I} $$
    Resistance (R) equals voltage (V) divided by current (I).

  • Example explanations:

    • Select points from voltage-current graph and calculate resistance using Ohm’s law.
    • Voltage difference calculation: (V_{AB} = V_A - V_B).
    • Current flows from higher to lower potential.
    • Current magnitude calculation:
      $$ I = \frac{V_{AB}}{R} $$

  • Resistor and heating wire example:

    • Heating wire resistance is 20 ohms, voltage 110V, current 5.5A.
    • When the wire length is halved, resistance halves, current doubles to 11A.
    • Increased current may cause the wire to burn out.

  • Multiple resistor current calculation:

    • Use node voltages to find voltage differences across resistors.
    • Apply the same current formula.

2-4.1 歐姆定律範例練習

重點歸納

  • 歐姆定律公式：
    $$ R = \frac{V}{I} $$
    電阻 (R) 等於電壓 (V) 除以電流 (I)。

  • 例題說明：

    • 從電壓與電流圖中選點，代入歐姆定律計算電阻值。
    • 電位差計算：兩點電位差 (V_{AB} = V_A - V_B)。
    • 電流方向由高電位流向低電位。
    • 電流大小計算：
      $$ I = \frac{V_{AB}}{R} $$

  • 電阻與電熱絲例題：

    • 電熱絲電阻為20歐姆，110伏特電壓，電流為5.5安培。
    • 電熱絲長度減半，電阻減半，電流變為11安培。
    • 電流增大可能導致電熱絲燒毀。

  • 多電阻電流計算：

    • 利用節點電位差計算各電阻電流。
    • 電流計算公式同上。

Basic Electricity 1 - Parallel Circuit Definition and Characteristics

Key Points

  1. Definition: A parallel circuit consists of two or more components, where one end of each component is connected together and the other end is also connected together.
  2. Characteristics:
    • Equal Voltage: The voltage across all components is the same.
    • Independence: If one component fails, it does not affect the operation of other components.
    • Total Resistance: The formula for total resistance ( R_T ) is:
      $$
      \frac{1}{R_T} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{R_n}
      $$
    • Conductance: The total conductance ( G_T ) is the sum of the conductances of each branch.

並聯電路定義及特性

重點歸納

  1. 定義：並聯電路由兩個或以上元件組成，所有元件的一端相連，另一端也相連。
  2. 特性：
    • 電壓相等：所有元件的電壓相同。
    • 獨立性：某一元件斷路不影響其他元件的運作。
    • 總電阻：總電阻 ( R_T ) 計算公式為：
      $$
      \frac{1}{R_T} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{R_n}
      $$
    • 電導：總電導 ( G_T ) 為各支路電導之和。

3-5 Examples of Series Circuit Applications

Key Points Summary

  • Voltmeter and Multiplier: The voltmeter measures a fixed voltage; adding a series multiplier resistor extends the measurement range.
  • Multiplier Principle: Series resistors adjust voltage division, allowing the voltmeter to measure higher voltages.
  • Multiplier Ratio Calculation: The multiplier ratio is the ratio of total measured voltage ( V_T ) to the voltmeter’s original voltage ( V_V ).
  • Example Calculation:
    • To measure 100V with a 10V full-scale voltmeter, a 9KΩ resistor is connected in series.
    • Multi-range voltmeters (10V, 100V, 250V) use different series resistors (9.9KΩ, 90KΩ, 150KΩ) to achieve measurement.
  • Ammeter Multiplier: Similar to the voltmeter, series resistors extend the measurement range.
  • Role of Series Resistors: Solve the problem of limited measurement range by voltage division.

3-5 串聯電路應用實例

重點歸納

  • 電壓表與倍增器：電壓表量測固定電壓，串聯倍增器（電阻）可擴大量測範圍。
  • 倍增器原理：串聯電阻調整電壓分壓，讓電壓表能量測更高電壓。
  • 倍增率計算：倍增率 = 待測總電壓 VT 與電壓表原始量測電壓 VV 的比值。
  • 實例計算：
    • 量測100伏特時，10伏特滿刻度電壓表需串聯9K歐姆電阻。
    • 多範圍電壓表（10V、100V、250V）透過串聯不同電阻（9.9K、90K、150K歐姆）達成量測。
  • 安培表倍增器：類似電壓表，透過串聯電阻擴大量測範圍。
  • 串聯電阻的作用：解決量測範圍不足的問題，透過分壓原理實現。

Title

DC Network Analysis: Basics and Problem-Solving Steps of Node Voltage Method (Part A)

Key Points Summary:

  • Node Definition: A point where two or more components connect.
  • Reference Node: Set as zero potential (ground).
  • Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL): The sum of currents entering a node equals the sum leaving it.
  • Ohm’s Law: Voltage across a resistor equals current times resistance.
  • Node Voltage Method Steps:
    1. Set reference node (0V).
    2. Label unknown node voltages.
    3. Assume current directions and label them.
    4. Write KCL equations.
    5. Use Ohm’s law to express currents.
    6. Substitute into KCL equations.
    7. Solve for unknown node voltages.
    8. Substitute back to find branch currents.
  • Negative Current: Indicates opposite direction to assumed.
  • Verification: Check current conservation.

標題

直流網路分析：節點電壓法基礎與解題步驟（Part A）

重點歸納

  • 節點定義：兩個或以上元件連接的點。
  • 參考節點：設為零電位（接地點）。
  • 克希荷夫電流定律 (KCL)：任一節點流入電流總和等於流出電流總和。
  • 歐姆定律：電阻兩端電壓差等於電流乘以電阻。
  • 節點電壓法步驟：
    1. 設定參考節點（0V）。
    2. 標示未知節點電壓。
    3. 假設電流方向並標示。
    4. 寫出KCL方程式。
    5. 利用歐姆定律寫出電流算式。
    6. 代入KCL方程式。
    7. 解方程式求未知節點電壓。
    8. 代回求各支路電流。
  • 電流負號意義：方向與假設相反。
  • 驗算：確認電流守恆。
