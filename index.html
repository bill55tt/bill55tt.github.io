Charged Particle Motion in Magnetic Fields: Force and Trajectory Analysis - 電荷在磁場中運動的受力與運動軌跡解析

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explores the fundamental principles of charged particle motion in magnetic fields, including the Lorentz force, trajectory analysis, and practical applications.

本文探討電荷在磁場中運動的基本原理，包括洛倫茲力、運動軌跡分析以及實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Lorentz Force and Direction 洛倫茲力與方向

  • A charged particle moving in a magnetic field experiences the Lorentz force, whose direction is determined by Fleming’s left-hand rule.

  • The direction of positive charge movement is the current direction; with the magnetic field directed into the plane, the force is perpendicular to both velocity and magnetic field.

  • 電荷在磁場中運動時會受到洛倫茲力作用，力的方向可用佛萊明左手定則判斷。

  • 正電荷運動方向為電流方向，磁場方向垂直紙面射入，受力方向垂直於速度與磁場方向。

2. Circular Motion 圓周運動

  • Positive charges undergo counterclockwise circular motion in the magnetic field, while negative charges move clockwise.

  • The force magnitude is given by ( F = B Q v ), where ( B ) is magnetic flux density, ( Q ) is charge, and ( v ) is velocity.

  • 電荷在磁場中會做逆時針圓周運動（正電荷），負電荷則為順時針圓周運動。

  • 受力大小公式為 ( F = B Q v )，其中 ( B ) 是磁通密度，( Q ) 是電荷量，( v ) 是速度。

3. Practical Applications 實際應用

  • This principle is applied in CRT displays to control electron beam deflection.

  • The understanding of charged particle motion in magnetic fields is crucial for various technologies including particle accelerators and mass spectrometers.

  • 此原理應用於CRT顯示器中控制電子束的偏轉。

  • 理解電荷在磁場中的運動對於粒子加速器和質譜儀等各種技術至關重要。

Analysis of Coupling Coefficient, Self-Inductance, and Mutual Inductance Relationships - 耦合係數、自感與互感關係式解析

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article analyzes the relationships between coupling coefficient, self-inductance, and mutual inductance in electromagnetic systems. It explains the fundamental concepts and mathematical relationships that govern these interactions.

本文分析電磁系統中耦合係數、自感和互感之間的關係。內容解釋這些相互作用的基本概念和數學關係。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Coupling Coefficient 耦合係數

  • Coupling coefficient K:

    • Represents the magnetic coupling efficiency between two coils
    • Ranges from 0 to 1
    • Closer to 1 indicates better coupling

  • 耦合係數K：

    • 表示兩線圈間磁通的導磁效率
    • 範圍為0到1
    • 越接近1表示耦合越好

2. Coupling Efficiencies 耦合效率

  • K1 and K2:

    • K1: Magnetic coupling efficiency from coil 1 to coil 2
    • K2: Magnetic coupling efficiency from coil 2 to coil 1
    • K1 = K2 in practice

  • K1與K2：

    • K1：線圈1對線圈2磁通的導磁效率
    • K2：線圈2對線圈1的導磁效率
    • 實際應用中K1 = K2

3. Magnetic Flux 磁通量

  • Magnetic flux relationships:

    • Φ12: Magnetic flux from coil 1 affecting coil 2
    • Φ21: Magnetic flux from coil 2 affecting coil 1

  • 磁通關係：

    • Φ12：線圈1產生影響線圈2的磁通
    • Φ21：線圈2影響線圈1的磁通

4. Self-Inductance 自感量

  • Self-inductance values:

    • L1: Inductance of coil 1
    • L2: Inductance of coil 2

  • 自感量：

    • L1：線圈1的電感量
    • L2：線圈2的電感量

5. Mutual Inductance 互感量

  • Mutual inductance formula:
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • Equality of mutual inductances:
    [
    M_{12} = M_{21} = M
    ]

  • 互感量公式：
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • 互感量相等關係：
    [
    M_{12} = M_{21} = M
    ]

Direction of Force on Current-Carrying Conductor in Magnetic Field and Fleming's Left-Hand Rule - 載流導體在磁場中所受力的方向與佛萊明左手定則

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the principles of force direction on current-carrying conductors in magnetic fields and the application of Fleming’s left-hand rule, including magnetic field interactions and practical applications.

本文解釋載流導體在磁場中受力方向的原理和佛萊明左手定則的應用，包括磁場相互作用和實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Current-Carrying Conductor and Magnetic Field 載流導體與磁場

  • Current-Carrying Conductor: A conductor with electric current generates a magnetic field.

  • Main Magnetic Field Direction: Magnetic lines run from the North (N) pole to the South (S) pole.

  • 載流導體：導體中通有電流，會產生磁場。

  • 主極磁場方向：磁力線由N極指向S極。

2. Magnetic Field Interactions 磁場相互作用

  • Interaction of Magnetic Lines:

    • Above the conductor, magnetic lines oppose and weaken each other.
    • Below the conductor, magnetic lines align and strengthen.

  • Force Direction: The conductor is pushed from regions of higher magnetic line density to lower density, resulting in an upward force.

  • 磁力線互動：

    • 導體上方磁力線方向相反，磁力線抵銷，磁場減弱。
    • 導體下方磁力線方向相同，磁力線增強。

  • 受力方向：導體受力由磁力線密度高處推向低處，導體向上受力。

3. Fleming’s Left-Hand Rule 佛萊明左手定則

  • Fleming’s Left-Hand Rule:

    • Thumb: Force direction (F)
    • Index finger: Magnetic field direction (B)
    • Middle finger: Current direction (I)
    • All three are perpendicular; use the left hand to determine the force direction on the conductor.

  • Application: Fleming’s left-hand rule simplifies determining the force direction on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field.

  • 佛萊明左手定則：

    • 拇指：受力方向（F）
    • 食指：磁場方向（B）
    • 中指：電流方向（I）
    • 三者互相垂直，用左手判斷載流導體受力方向。

  • 應用：佛萊明左手定則簡化判斷載流導體在磁場中受力方向的過程。

Magnitude of Force on a Current-Carrying Conductor in a Magnetic Field - 載流導體在磁場中所受力的大小

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article examines the calculation of force magnitude on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field, including both perpendicular and angled cases, along with practical examples.

本文探討載流導體在磁場中受力大小的計算方法，包括垂直和斜角兩種情況，並提供實際範例。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Force Calculation Methods 受力計算方法

  • The force magnitude on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field is calculated in two cases:

    1. Conductor perpendicular to magnetic field:
      Formula: ( F = B l I )
      where ( F ) is force (Newtons), ( B ) is magnetic flux density (Tesla), ( l ) is effective length of conductor in the field (meters), and ( I ) is current (Amperes).
    2. Conductor at an angle (\theta) to magnetic field:
      Formula: ( F = B l I \sin \theta ), where (\sin \theta) adjusts the effective length cut by the magnetic field.

  • 載流導體在磁場中受力的大小計算分兩種情況：

    1. 導體與磁場垂直：
      公式為 ( F = B l I )
      其中，
      • ( F ) 是受力大小（牛頓，N）
      • ( B ) 是磁通密度（特斯拉，T）
      • ( l ) 是導體在磁場中的有效長度（公尺，m）
      • ( I ) 是電流大小（安培，A）
    2. 導體與磁場不垂直（與磁場成角度 (\theta)）：
      公式修正為 ( F = B l I \sin \theta )，其中 (\sin \theta) 是導體在磁場中切割的有效長度比例。

2. Force Direction 力的方向

  • The force direction is determined by Fleming’s left-hand rule:
    Thumb points to force, index finger to magnetic field, middle finger to current.

  • 力的方向可用佛萊明左手定則判斷：
    拇指指向力的方向，食指指向磁場方向，中指指向電流方向。

3. Example Calculations 範例計算

  • Example calculations:

    • Perpendicular case: ( B=2,T, l=1,m, I=10,A \Rightarrow F=20,N )
    • Angled case: ( B=0.5,T, l=0.2,m, I=10,A, \theta=30^\circ \Rightarrow F=0.5,N )

  • 範例計算：

    • 垂直情況：( B=2,T, l=1,m, I=10,A \Rightarrow F=20,N )
    • 斜角情況：( B=0.5,T, l=0.2,m, I=10,A, \theta=30^\circ \Rightarrow F=0.5,N )

High School Basic Electricity: Electromagnetic Effect Part A - 高中基本電學：電磁效應 PART A

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explores the fundamental concepts of electromagnetic effects, including the relationship between electricity and magnetism, practical applications, and key principles.

本文探討電磁效應的基本概念，包括電與磁的關係、實際應用和重要原理。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Electromagnetic Effect 電磁效應

  • Electromagnetic effect refers to the phenomenon where an electric current produces a magnetic field (electricity generates magnetism).

  • This fundamental principle is the basis for many electrical devices and applications.

  • 電磁效應是電流產生磁場的現象（電生磁）。

  • 這個基本原理是許多電氣設備和應用的基礎。

2. Hall Effect and Applications 霍爾效應與應用

  • The Hall effect sensor converts magnetic energy into electrical energy.

  • Applications include:

    • Smartphone smart covers
    • RFID technology in transportation cards (e.g., EasyCard)
    • Various proximity sensors

  • 霍爾效應感測器利用磁場轉換成電能。

  • 應用包括：

    • 手機智能皮套
    • 悠遊卡的RFID技術
    • 各種接近感測器

3. Magnetic Field Direction and Right-Hand Rule 磁場方向與安培右手定則

  • The magnetic field direction around a current-carrying conductor can be determined by the right-hand rule:

    • Current into the paper produces a clockwise magnetic field
    • Current out of the paper produces a counterclockwise magnetic field

  • 載流導體周圍磁場方向可用安培右手定則判定：

    • 電流流入紙面磁場為順時針
    • 電流流出紙面為逆時針

4. Electromagnetic Induction 電磁感應

  • Electromagnetic induction is the effect where a magnetic field induces an electric current.

  • When a coil moves in a magnetic field, current is induced.

  • This principle is the foundation of generator operation.

  • 電磁感應是磁生電的效應。

  • 線圈在磁場中運動會感應出電流。

  • 這是發電機運作的理論基礎。

High School Basic Electricity: Electromagnetic Induction Examples - 高中基本電學：電磁感應範例解析

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article presents various examples of Faraday’s electromagnetic induction law, including basic problems, self-inductance calculations, and magnetic flux density applications.

本文介紹法拉第電磁感應定律的各種範例，包括基本題型、自感計算和磁通密度應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Faraday’s Law Problem 法拉第電磁感應基本題型

  • Key parameters: number of coil turns (N), change in magnetic flux (\Delta \Phi), and time interval (\Delta t).

  • Induced EMF formula:
    $$
    e = N \frac{\Delta \Phi}{\Delta t}
    $$

  • Example: 5000 turns, flux changes from 0.01Wb to 0.03Wb in 0.5s, EMF = 200V.

  • 線圈匝數 (N)、磁通變化量 (\Delta \Phi)、時間間隔 (\Delta t) 是計算感應電勢的關鍵參數。

  • 感應電勢公式：
    $$
    e = N \frac{\Delta \Phi}{\Delta t}
    $$

  • 範例：5000匝線圈，磁通0.01Wb增加到0.03Wb，0.5秒內，感應電勢為200V。

2. Advanced Problem: Self-Inductance and Current Change 進階題型：自感電感與電流變化

  • Parameters: inductance (L), current change (\Delta i), time interval (\Delta t).

  • Formula:
    $$
    e = L \frac{\Delta i}{\Delta t}
    $$

  • Example: 4H inductance, current increases from 2A to 7A in 2s, EMF = 10V.

  • 自感電感 (L)、電流變化量 (\Delta i)、時間間隔 (\Delta t) 用於計算感應電勢。

  • 公式：
    $$
    e = L \frac{\Delta i}{\Delta t}
    $$

  • 範例：4H電感，電流2秒由2A增至7A，感應電勢為10V。

3. Calculating Flux Change from Magnetic Flux Density and Area 利用磁通密度與面積計算磁通變化

  • Magnetic flux change:
    $$
    \Delta \Phi = B \times A
    $$

  • Example: 10 turns, magnetic flux density 2T, area 0.25㎡, time 0.1s, EMF = 50V.

  • 磁通密度 (B) 與磁極面積 (A) 乘積得磁通變化量：
    $$
    \Delta \Phi = B \times A
    $$

  • 例：10匝線圈，磁通密度2T，面積0.25㎡，0.1秒內感應電勢為50V。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Introduction to Electromagnetic Induction 電磁感應介紹

  • Introduction to Electromagnetic Induction: Explains Faraday’s experiment and law, showing how changing magnetic fields induce current in a conductor.

  • 電磁感應介紹：介紹法拉第電磁感應實驗與定律，說明磁場變化如何在導線中產生感應電流。

2. Experimental Phenomenon 實驗現象

  • Experimental Phenomenon: When a magnet is quickly inserted or removed from a coil, the galvanometer deflects, indicating induced current; no current when stationary.

  • Induced Current Magnitude: Proportional to the speed of magnet movement; faster movement means larger induced current.

  • 實驗現象：磁鐵快速插入或抽出線圈時，檢流計會偏轉，顯示有感應電流產生；靜止時無感應電流。

  • 感應電流大小：與磁鐵插入或抽出速度成正比，速度越快感應電流越大。

3. Mathematical Relationships 數學關係

  • Induced EMF Formula:
    $$
    e = N \frac{\Delta \Phi}{\Delta t}
    $$
    where (e) is induced EMF, (N) is coil turns, and (\frac{\Delta \Phi}{\Delta t}) is the rate of change of magnetic flux.

  • 感應電動勢公式：
    $$
    e = N \frac{\Delta \Phi}{\Delta t}
    $$
    其中 (e) 是感應電動勢，(N) 是線圈匝數，(\frac{\Delta \Phi}{\Delta t}) 是磁通量變化率。

4. Inductance and Induced EMF 電感與感應電動勢

  • Inductance and Induced EMF:
    $$
    e = L \frac{\Delta i}{\Delta t}
    $$
    where (L) is inductance and (\frac{\Delta i}{\Delta t}) is the rate of current change; both formulas are equivalent.

  • 電感與感應電動勢：
    $$
    e = L \frac{\Delta i}{\Delta t}
    $$
    (L) 是電感量，(\frac{\Delta i}{\Delta t}) 是電流變化率，兩公式等價。

Example of Force Between Two Long Straight Conductors Carrying Current - 兩長直導體通電流所受之力範例解析

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article examines the magnetic force between two long straight conductors carrying current, including the calculation formula and practical examples with different scenarios.

本文探討兩長直導體通電流時彼此間的磁力作用，包括計算公式和不同情況下的實際範例。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Principles 基本原理

  • Two long straight conductors carrying current exert magnetic forces on each other.

  • Force magnitude formula:
    $$
    F = \frac{\mu l I_1 I_2}{2 \pi d}
    $$
    where $\mu$ is permeability, $l$ is length, $I_1, I_2$ are currents, and $d$ is distance between conductors.

  • 兩長直導體通電流時，彼此會產生磁力作用。

  • 受力大小公式為：
    $$
    F = \frac{\mu l I_1 I_2}{2 \pi d}
    $$
    其中，$\mu$為磁導率，$l$為導體長度，$I_1, I_2$為電流，$d$為兩導體間距。

2. Force Direction 力的方向

  • Same current direction causes attraction; opposite direction causes repulsion.

  • The direction of force can be determined using the right-hand rule for magnetic fields.

  • 電流方向相同時，導體間力為相吸；方向相反時，力為相斥。

  • 力的方向可用磁場的右手定則判斷。

3. Example Calculations 範例計算

  • Example 1: $I_1=10A$, $I_2=20A$, $l=10m$, $d=1m$, force is $4 \times 10^{-4} N$, attractive.

  • Example 2: Two wires 0.5m apart, current 20A, length 50m, force is 0.008N, repulsive.

  • 範例1：$I_1=10A$, $I_2=20A$, $l=10m$, $d=1m$，計算出力為 $4 \times 10^{-4} N$，方向相吸。

  • 範例2：電路中兩條線距離0.5m，電流20A，長度50m，計算力為0.008N，方向相斥。

High School Basic Electricity: Inductance Calculations Part B - 高中基本電學：電感量計算 PART B

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article presents Part B of the inductance calculations series, focusing on basic inductance calculations with practical examples. It demonstrates how to calculate inductance using different formulas and parameters.

本文介紹電感量計算系列的B部分，重點在於基本電感計算的實際範例。內容展示如何使用不同的公式和參數來計算電感量。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Example 1: Basic Inductance Calculation 範例一：基本電感量計算

  • Given parameters:

    • Number of turns (N) = 100
    • Current (I) = 10 A
