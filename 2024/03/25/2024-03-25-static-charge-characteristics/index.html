High School Basic Electricity: Characteristics of Static Charge and Example Exercises - 高中基本電學：靜電荷的特性與範例練習

Posted on 2024-03-25 Edited on 2025-05-05 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Views: Disqus:

Introduction

Static charge is a fundamental concept in electricity that helps us understand the behavior of electric charges at rest. This article explores the basic characteristics of static charge, including the concepts of electrons and coulombs, and provides practical examples to enhance understanding.

引言

靜電荷是電學中的基本概念，幫助我們理解靜止狀態下電荷的行為。本文將探討靜電荷的基本特性，包括電子和庫侖的概念，並提供實際範例以加深理解。

Basic Unit of Electric Charge

The fundamental unit of electric charge is the coulomb (C)

One electron carries a charge of -1.6 × 10^-19 coulombs

One proton carries a charge of +1.6 × 10^-19 coulombs

The charge of an electron is equal in magnitude but opposite in sign to that of a proton

電量的基本單位

電量的基本單位是庫侖(C)

一個電子帶有-1.6 × 10^-19庫侖的電量

一個質子帶有+1.6 × 10^-19庫侖的電量

電子的電量與質子大小相等但符號相反

Definition of Static Charge

Static charge refers to electric charge that is at rest

It is the accumulation of electric charge on the surface of an object

Static charge can be generated through friction, contact, or induction

The charge remains stationary until it is discharged or neutralized

靜電荷的定義

靜電荷指的是靜止不動的電荷

它是物體表面電荷的累積

靜電荷可以通過摩擦、接觸或感應產生

電荷保持靜止直到被放電或中和

Characteristics of Static Charge

Like Charges Repel

Charges of the same type repel each other

Positive charges repel other positive charges

Negative charges repel other negative charges

This is a fundamental property of electric charges

Opposite Charges Attract

Charges of different types attract each other

Positive and negative charges are attracted to each other

This attraction is the basis of many electrical phenomena

The force of attraction increases with decreasing distance

Electrostatic Induction

Neutral objects can be affected by nearby charged objects

Charges in the neutral object redistribute themselves

This creates temporary charge separation

The effect is stronger at closer distances

靜電荷的特性

同性相斥

相同類型的電荷互相排斥

正電荷排斥其他正電荷

負電荷排斥其他負電荷

這是電荷的基本特性

異性相吸

不同類型的電荷互相吸引

正電荷和負電荷互相吸引

這種吸引力是許多電學現象的基礎

吸引力隨距離減少而增加

靜電感應

中性物體會受到附近帶電物體的影響

中性物體中的電荷會重新分布

這會產生暫時的電荷分離

距離越近，效果越強

Franklin's Experiment

Benjamin Franklin conducted experiments with static electricity

He rubbed a glass rod with silk to generate static charge

The glass rod became positively charged

The silk became negatively charged

This experiment demonstrated the transfer of electrons

富蘭克林的實驗

班傑明・富蘭克林進行了靜電實驗

他通過摩擦玻璃棒和絲綢產生靜電

玻璃棒帶正電

絲綢帶負電

這個實驗展示了電子的轉移

Example Exercises

Example 1: Calculating Atomic Charge

Calculate the electric charge of an atom with 6 electrons and 3 protons:

Q = -6 × 1.6 × 10^-19 + 3 × 1.6 × 10^-19

Q = -4.8 × 10^-19 C

The atom has a net negative charge

Example 2: Charge Removal

Calculate the final charge after removing -1.5 microcoulombs:

Initial charge = -1.5 × 10^-6 C

Removed charge = -1.5 × 10^-6 C

Final charge = 0 C (neutral)

範例練習

範例1：計算原子電量

計算帶有6個電子和3個質子的原子電量：

Q = -6 × 1.6 × 10^-19 + 3 × 1.6 × 10^-19

Q = -4.8 × 10^-19 C

該原子帶有淨負電荷

範例2：電荷移除

計算移去-1.5微庫侖電荷後的帶電量：

初始電量 = -1.5 × 10^-6 C

移除電量 = -1.5 × 10^-6 C

最終電量 = 0 C（中性）

Summary

Static charge is electric charge at rest

Like charges repel, opposite charges attract

Electrostatic induction affects neutral objects

Charge can be calculated using the number of electrons and protons

Understanding static charge is fundamental to electricity

總結

靜電荷是靜止的電荷

同性相斥，異性相吸

靜電感應影響中性物體

電量可以通過電子和質子數量計算

理解靜電荷是電學的基礎
