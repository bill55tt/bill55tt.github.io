High School Basic Electricity: Fundamental Theories of Electricity - Charge Part A - 高中基本電學：電學基礎理論 - 電荷 PART A

Posted on 2024-03-25 Edited on 2025-05-05 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Views: Disqus:

Introduction

The study of electricity begins with understanding the fundamental concept of electric charge. This article explores the historical development of charge theory and introduces key scientific contributions that shaped our understanding of electricity.

引言

電學的研究始於理解電荷的基本概念。本文探討電荷理論的歷史發展，並介紹塑造我們對電學理解的關鍵科學貢獻。

Historical Background

The understanding of electric charge has evolved through the contributions of several important scientists:

Sir Isaac Newton (1643-1727): His work on universal gravitation influenced later theories of electrical forces

Charles-Augustin de Coulomb (1736-1806): Formulated Coulomb's law describing electrical forces

John Dalton (1766-1844): Proposed atomic theory, though later modified

Robert Millikan (1868-1953): Measured the charge of a single electron

Ernest Rutherford (1871-1937): Contributed to understanding atomic structure

歷史背景

對電荷的理解通過幾位重要科學家的貢獻而發展：

艾薩克・牛頓爵士(1643-1727)：他對萬有引力的研究影響了後來的電力理論

查爾斯・奧古斯丁・庫侖(1736-1806)：提出描述電力作用的庫侖定律

約翰・道耳吞(1766-1844)：提出原子理論，後被修正

羅伯特・密利根(1868-1953)：測量單一電子的電荷

歐內斯特・拉塞福(1871-1937)：對理解原子結構做出貢獻

Basic Charge and Electric Quantity

The fundamental unit of electric charge is the coulomb (C)

One electron carries a charge of -1.6 × 10^-19 coulombs

One proton carries a charge of +1.6 × 10^-19 coulombs

The charge of an electron is equal in magnitude but opposite in sign to that of a proton

基本電荷與電量

電量的基本單位是庫侖(C)

一個電子帶有-1.6 × 10^-19庫侖的電量

一個質子帶有+1.6 × 10^-19庫侖的電量

電子的電量與質子大小相等但符號相反

Key Scientific Contributions

Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation

Influenced the development of Coulomb's law

Demonstrated the concept of action at a distance

Established mathematical relationships for forces

Coulomb's Law

Describes the force between two point charges

Like charges repel, unlike charges attract

Force is proportional to the product of charges

Force is inversely proportional to the square of distance

Dalton's Atomic Theory

Atoms are indivisible (later revised)

All atoms of an element are identical

Atoms combine in simple whole number ratios

Millikan's Oil Drop Experiment

Measured the charge of a single electron

Determined the basic charge unit

Confirmed the quantization of electric charge

關鍵科學貢獻

牛頓的萬有引力定律

影響了庫侖定律的發展

展示了超距作用的概念

建立了力的數學關係

庫侖定律

描述兩個點電荷之間的力

同性相斥，異性相吸

力與電荷乘積成正比

力與距離平方成反比

道耳吞的原子理論

原子是不可分割的（後被修正）

同一元素的所有原子都相同

原子以簡單整數比結合

密利根的油滴實驗

測量單一電子的電荷

確定了基本電荷單位

證實了電荷的量子化

Charge Calculations

Example 1: Total Charge Calculation

Calculate the total charge of 5 × 10^19 electrons:

Q = 5 × 10^19 × (-1.6 × 10^-19)

Q = -8 coulombs

The negative sign indicates excess electrons

Example 2: Number of Electrons

Calculate the number of electrons in 1 coulomb:

Number of electrons = 1 / (1.6 × 10^-19)

Number of electrons = 6.25 × 10^18

電荷計算

範例1：總電量計算

計算5 × 10^19個電子的總電量：

Q = 5 × 10^19 × (-1.6 × 10^-19)

Q = -8庫侖

負號表示過量的電子

範例2：電子數量

計算1庫侖中的電子數量：

電子數量 = 1 / (1.6 × 10^-19)

電子數量 = 6.25 × 10^18

Summary

Electric charge is a fundamental property of matter

The basic unit of charge is the coulomb

Charges interact through Coulomb's law

Understanding charge is essential for electricity

Historical experiments shaped our understanding

總結

電荷是物質的基本屬性

電量的基本單位是庫侖

電荷通過庫侖定律相互作用

理解電荷對電學至關重要

歷史實驗塑造了我們的理解
