High School Basic Electricity: Series Inductance Calculation Examples - 高中基本電學：串聯總電感運算範例

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in series circuits, both with and without considering mutual inductance effects. It includes practical examples and formulas for different scenarios.

本文解釋串聯電路中總電感的計算方法，包括考慮和不考慮互感效應的情況。內容包含實際範例和不同情況下的計算公式。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Series Inductance Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的串聯電感計算

  • Total inductance is the sum of each inductance

  • Similar to resistors in series

  • Example: 12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

  • 總電感為各電感直接相加

  • 類似電阻串聯計算

  • 範例：12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

2. Series Inductance With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的串聯電感計算

  • For three inductors (L_1, L_2, L_3) in series

  • Consider mutual inductances (M_{12}, M_{23}, M_{31})

  • Total inductance formula:
    $$
    L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + L_3 \pm 2M_{12} \pm 2M_{23} \pm 2M_{31}
    $$

  • Sign convention:

    • Plus sign for mutual aiding (same magnetic flux direction)
    • Minus sign for mutual opposing (opposite magnetic flux direction)

  • 三個電感 (L_1, L_2, L_3) 串聯

  • 考慮互感 (M_{12}, M_{23}, M_{31})

  • 總電感公式：
    $$
    L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + L_3 \pm 2M_{12} \pm 2M_{23} \pm 2M_{31}
    $$

  • 符號規則：

    • 互助（磁力線方向相同）取加號
    • 互消（磁力線方向相反）取減號

3. Example Calculations 範例計算

  • Without mutual inductance:

    • Simple addition of individual inductances
    • Example: 12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

  • With mutual inductance:

    • Determine aiding or opposing by magnetic flux direction
    • Apply appropriate signs in the formula
    • Calculate total inductance

  • 不考慮互感：

    • 直接相加各電感值
    • 範例：12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

  • 考慮互感：

    • 根據磁力線方向判斷互助或互消
    • 代入公式計算
    • 得出總電感值

Series Combination of Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 電感器串聯組合：有互感與無互感

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in series inductor combinations, covering both cases with and without mutual inductance. It includes formulas and practical considerations for different scenarios.

本文解釋電感器串聯組合的計算方法，包括有互感與無互感的情況。內容包含不同情況下的計算公式和實際考量。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Inductors in Series Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的串聯電感

  • The total inductance is the sum of the individual inductances:
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2$$

  • This is the basic formula for series inductors without mutual effects

  • 總電感為各電感直接相加：
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2$$

  • 這是不考慮互感時串聯電感的基本公式

2. Inductors in Series With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的串聯電感

  • Need to determine if mutual effect is:

    • Aiding (same magnetic field direction)
    • Opposing (opposite magnetic field direction)

  • The mutual inductance term affects the total inductance calculation

  • 需判斷互感是：

    • 互助（磁場方向相同）
    • 互消（磁場方向相反）

  • 互感項會影響總電感的計算

3. Series Aiding Configuration 串聯互助配置

  • The magnetic fields have the same direction

  • Mutual inductance is positive

  • Total inductance formula:
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M$$

  • 磁場方向相同

  • 互感為正值

  • 總電感公式：
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M$$

4. Series Opposing Configuration 串聯互消配置

  • The magnetic fields have opposite directions

  • Mutual inductance is negative

  • Total inductance formula:
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 - 2M$$

  • 磁場方向相反

  • 互感為負值

  • 總電感公式：
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 - 2M$$

5. Polarity Marking (N-dot Convention) 極性標記（N點標示法）

  • Use dots to indicate the polarity (N-pole direction) of the coils

  • Helps determine whether mutual inductance is aiding or opposing

  • Important for correct calculation of total inductance

  • 使用點來標示線圈的極性（N極方向）

  • 有助於判斷互感是互助或互消

  • 對正確計算總電感很重要

Magnetic Field Intensity of a Solenoid - 螺線管的磁場強度

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental concepts of magnetic field intensity in solenoids, including the calculation of magnetomotive force and the principles of magnetic field superposition.

本文解釋螺線管中磁場強度的基本概念，包括磁動勢的計算和磁場疊加原理。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Magnetomotive Force (MMF) 磁動勢

  • Definition:

    • Energy of magnetic flux generated by current
    • Formula: MMF = ( NI )
    • Where N is number of turns, I is current

  • 定義：

    • 電流產生的磁力線能量
    • 公式：磁動勢 = ( NI )
    • N為匝數，I為電流

2. Magnetic Field Intensity 磁場強度

  • Calculation Formula:
    $$
    H = \frac{NI}{L}
    $$

    • H: magnetic field intensity
    • N: number of turns
    • I: current
    • L: magnetic path length

  • 計算公式：
    $$
    H = \frac{NI}{L}
    $$

    • H：磁場強度
    • N：匝數
    • I：電流
    • L：磁路長度

3. Units 單位

  • Standard Unit:

    • Ampere-turns per meter (A·t/m)
    • Represents magnetic field strength
    • International standard unit

  • 標準單位：

    • 安匝/公尺（A·t/m）
    • 表示磁場強度
    • 國際標準單位

4. Field Superposition 磁場疊加

  • Principle:

    • Multiple coils’ fields can be added
    • Vector addition for same direction
    • Vector subtraction for opposite directions

  • 原理：

    • 多個線圈的磁場可以相加
    • 同向時做向量加法
    • 反向時做向量減法

5. Practical Applications 實際應用

  • Electromagnetic Devices:

    • Solenoids
    • Electromagnets
    • Transformers
    • Magnetic sensors

  • 電磁裝置：

    • 螺線管
    • 電磁鐵
    • 變壓器
    • 磁感測器

6. Important Considerations 重要考量

  • Factors Affecting Field Strength:

    • Number of turns
    • Current magnitude
    • Core material
    • Path length

  • 影響磁場強度的因素：

    • 匝數
    • 電流大小
    • 心材材料
    • 路徑長度

High School Basic Electricity: Conductance and Conductance Coefficient - 高中基本電學：電導與電導係數

Posted on 2025-05-05 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article introduces the fundamental concepts of conductance and conductance coefficient, including their definitions, mathematical relationships, and the factors that affect conductance in materials.

引言

本文介紹電導與電導係數的基本概念，包括其定義、數學關係以及影響材料電導的因素。

Key Points

  1. Definition of Conductance:
    Conductance describes the ability of a material to allow charged particles to flow, which is the opposite of resistance.
  2. Relationship Between Conductance and Resistance:
    Conductance \( G \) and resistance \( R \) are reciprocals:
    $$ G = \frac{1}{R} $$
    Conductance formula:
    $$ G = \gamma \cdot \frac{A}{L} $$
    Where \( \gamma \) is the conductance coefficient, \( A \) is the cross-sectional area, and \( L \) is the length of the object.
  3. Factors Affecting Conductance:
    Conductance Coefficient (\( \gamma \)): The conductivity of the material.
    Length of the Object (\( L \)): Longer lengths result in lower conductance.
    Cross-sectional Area (\( A \)): Larger areas result in higher conductance.
  4. Percentage Conductance Coefficient:
    Used to compare the conductivity of materials against standard copper.

重點歸納

  1. 電導的定義：
    電導是描述物體允許帶電粒子流動的能力，與電阻相反。
  2. 電導與電阻的關係：
    電導 \( G \) 與電阻 \( R \) 互為倒數：
    $$ G = \frac{1}{R} $$
    電導公式：
    $$ G = \gamma \cdot \frac{A}{L} $$
    其中，\( \gamma \) 是電導係數，\( A \) 是截面積，\( L \) 是物體長度。
  3. 影響電導的因素：
    電導係數（\( \gamma \)）：材質的導電能力。
    物體長度（\( L \)）：長度越長，電導越小。
    截面積（\( A \)）：截面積越大，電導越大。
  4. 百分率電導係數：
    用於比較材料的導電能力，以標準韌銅為基準。

High School Basic Electricity: Parallel Combination of Inductors (Without Mutual Inductance, With Mutual Inductance) - 高中基本電學：電感器並聯組合(無互感、互感)

Posted on 2025-05-05 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article introduces the calculation methods for parallel combinations of inductors, including both cases without mutual inductance and with mutual inductance. It also explains how the direction of mutual inductance (assisting or opposing) affects the total inductance.

引言

本文介紹電感器並聯組合的計算方法，包含無互感與有互感兩種情況，並說明互感方向（互助或互消）對總電感量的影響。

Key Points

  1. Parallel Inductors Without Mutual Inductance:
    The formula is similar to parallel resistors:
    $$L_T = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}$$
  2. Parallel Inductors With Mutual Inductance:
    Determine if the mutual effect is assisting (same magnetic field direction) or opposing (opposite magnetic field direction).
    The formula is:
    $$L_T = \frac{L_1 L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 \mp 2M}$$
    Use minus sign (−2M) for assisting mutual inductance (larger total inductance).
    Use plus sign (+2M) for opposing mutual inductance (smaller total inductance).

重點歸納

  1. 無互感並聯電感：
    計算公式與電阻並聯相似：
    $$L_T = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}$$
  2. 有互感並聯電感：
    需判斷互感是互助（磁場方向相同）或互消（磁場方向相反）。
    公式：
    $$L_T = \frac{L_1 L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 \mp 2M}$$
    互助取負號（−2M），電感量較大。
    互消取正號（+2M），電感量較小。

    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M$$

    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2$$

    需判斷互助（助磁）或互消（消磁）。
    串聯互助：
    磁場方向相同，互感為正。
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M$$
    串聯互消：
    磁場方向相反，互感為負。
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 - 2M$$

Introduction

引言

Key Points

    $$L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}$$
    Example: For L1=8H, L2=2H, and M=2H, total inductance is 2H.
  3. Mutual Inductance Direction (Dot Convention):
    The direction of mutual inductance (dot convention) affects the calculation. Head-to-head and tail-to-tail with dots in the same direction is mutual assistance in parallel.

  2. 並聯電感計算（考慮互感）：
    $$L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}$$
    例：L1=8H，L2=2H，M=2H，總電感為2H。
  3. 互感方向（打點法則）：
    互感方向（打點法則）會影響計算，頭接頭尾接尾且打點同向屬於互助並聯。

High School Basic Electricity: Parallel Circuits - 高中基本電學：並聯電路

Posted on 2025-05-05 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article explores the fundamental concepts of parallel circuits, including their definition, characteristics, and mathematical relationships. Understanding parallel circuits is essential for analyzing complex electrical systems and designing efficient circuits.

引言

本文探討並聯電路的基本概念，包括其定義、特性以及數學關係。理解並聯電路對於分析複雜電氣系統和設計高效電路至關重要。

Key Points

Parallel Circuit Definition and Characteristics

  1. Definition:
    A parallel circuit consists of two or more components, where one end of each component is connected together and the other end is also connected together.
  2. Characteristics:
    Equal Voltage: The voltage across all components is the same.
    Independence: If one component fails, it does not affect the operation of other components.
    Total Resistance: The formula for total resistance \( R_T \) is:
    $$\frac{1}{R_T} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{R_n}$$
    Conductance: The total conductance \( G_T \) is the sum of the conductances of each branch.

重點歸納

並聯電路定義及特性

  1. 定義：
    並聯電路由兩個或以上元件組成，所有元件的一端相連，另一端也相連。
  2. 特性：
    電壓相等：所有元件的電壓相同。
    獨立性：某一元件斷路不影響其他元件的運作。
    總電阻：總電阻 \( R_T \) 計算公式為：
    $$\frac{1}{R_T} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{R_n}$$
    電導：總電導 \( G_T \) 為各支路電導之和。

Key Points

  1. Power
    Definition: The work done or energy used per unit time.
    Formula: $P = \frac{W}{t}$
    Unit: Watt (W)
  2. Horsepower
    Definition: The power required to lift a 76 kg weight by 1 meter in 1 second.
    Unit: Horsepower (hp)
    Conversion: 1 hp = 746 W
  3. Electric Power
    Definition: The product of voltage and current in a circuit.
    Formula: $P = VI$
    Unit: Watt (W)
  4. Examples
    • Example 1: A lamp has a potential difference of 12 V and a current of 5 A. What is the power consumed?
      $P = VI = 12 \times 5 = 60\,W$
    • Example 2: A prime mover lifts a 1000 N weight by 20 m in 4 seconds. What is the output power and its horsepower?
      Work $W = FS = 1000 \times 20 = 20,000\,J$
      Power $P = \frac{W}{t} = \frac{20,000}{4} = 5,000\,W = 5\,kW$
      Horsepower $= \frac{5,000}{746} \approx 6.7\,hp$

詳細重點統整

  1. 功率
    定義：單位時間內所做的功或能量。
    公式：$P = \frac{W}{t}$
    單位：瓦特 (Watt, W)
  2. 馬力
    定義：一匹馬在1秒鐘內將76公斤的重物提高1公尺。
    單位：馬力 (Horsepower, hp)
    換算：1 hp = 746 W
  3. 電功率
    定義：電路中電壓和電流的乘積。
    公式：$P = VI$
    單位：瓦特 (Watt, W)
  4. 範例
    • 範例一：電燈兩端的電位差12伏特，流過電燈的電流5安培，消耗功率多少瓦特？
      $P = VI = 12 \times 5 = 60\,W$
    • 範例二：有一個原動機在4秒鐘將1000牛頓的重物提高20公尺，輸出功率多少瓦還有它的馬力是多少？
      做功 $W = FS = 1000 \times 20 = 20,000\,J$
      功率 $P = \frac{W}{t} = \frac{20,000}{4} = 5,000\,W = 5\,kW$
      馬力 $= \frac{5,000}{746} \approx 6.7\,hp$
