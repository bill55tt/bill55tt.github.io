Self-Inductance and Mutual Inductance - 自感與互感

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-17 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental concepts of self-inductance and mutual inductance in electromagnetic systems. It covers the basic principles, mathematical relationships, and practical applications of these important electromagnetic phenomena.

本文解釋電磁系統中自感和互感的基本概念。內容涵蓋這些重要電磁現象的基本原理、數學關係和實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Self-Inductance 自感

  • Definition:

    • The magnetic flux generated by current flowing through a single coil
    • Related to the coil’s own current
    • A fundamental property of any coil

  • 定義：

    • 單一線圈通電時產生的磁通量
    • 與該線圈自身的電流相關
    • 任何線圈的基本特性

2. Mutual Inductance 互感

  • Definition:

    • Occurs when two coils are in proximity
    • Current in one coil produces magnetic flux that links to the other coil
    • Denoted by capital M

  • 定義：

    • 當兩個相鄰線圈存在時發生
    • 一個線圈的電流會產生磁通量，並交鏈到另一個線圈
    • 表示為大寫M

3. Magnetic Flux Components 磁通量分解

  • Flux in coil 1:

    • Total flux Φ1 splits into:
      • Φ11: Leakage flux (not linking the other coil)
      • Φ12: Mutual flux (linking the other coil)

  • 線圈1的磁通：

    • 總磁通Φ1分為：
      • Φ11：漏磁通（未交鏈到另一線圈）
      • Φ12：交鏈磁通（穿過另一線圈）

4. Self-Inductance Formula 自感量公式

  • Formula for coil 1:
    [
    L_1 = \frac{N_1 \Phi_1}{I_1}
    ]
    where:

    • N1: Number of turns
    • I1: Current

  • 線圈1的公式：
    [
    L_1 = \frac{N_1 \Phi_1}{I_1}
    ]
    其中：

    • N1：線圈匝數
    • I1：電流

5. Mutual Inductance Formula 互感量公式

  • Formula for mutual inductance:
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}
    ]
    This represents the mutual inductance from coil 1 to coil 2

  • 互感量公式：
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}
    ]
    表示線圈1對線圈2的互感

6. Coil 2 Relationships 線圈2的關係

  • Similar definitions apply to coil 2:

    • Self-inductance L2
    • Mutual inductance M21

  • 線圈2也有類似的定義：

    • 自感量L2
    • 互感量M21

High School Basic Electricity: Series Inductance Calculation Examples - 高中基本電學：串聯總電感運算範例

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in series circuits, both with and without considering mutual inductance effects. It includes practical examples and formulas for different scenarios.

本文解釋串聯電路中總電感的計算方法，包括考慮和不考慮互感效應的情況。內容包含實際範例和不同情況下的計算公式。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Series Inductance Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的串聯電感計算

  • Total inductance is the sum of each inductance

  • Similar to resistors in series

  • Example: 12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

  • 總電感為各電感直接相加

  • 類似電阻串聯計算

  • 範例：12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

2. Series Inductance With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的串聯電感計算

  • For three inductors (L_1, L_2, L_3) in series

  • Consider mutual inductances (M_{12}, M_{23}, M_{31})

  • Total inductance formula:
    $$
    L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + L_3 \pm 2M_{12} \pm 2M_{23} \pm 2M_{31}
    $$

  • Sign convention:

    • Plus sign for mutual aiding (same magnetic flux direction)
    • Minus sign for mutual opposing (opposite magnetic flux direction)

  • 三個電感 (L_1, L_2, L_3) 串聯

  • 考慮互感 (M_{12}, M_{23}, M_{31})

  • 總電感公式：
    $$
    L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + L_3 \pm 2M_{12} \pm 2M_{23} \pm 2M_{31}
    $$

  • 符號規則：

    • 互助（磁力線方向相同）取加號
    • 互消（磁力線方向相反）取減號

3. Example Calculations 範例計算

  • Without mutual inductance:

    • Simple addition of individual inductances
    • Example: 12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

  • With mutual inductance:

    • Determine aiding or opposing by magnetic flux direction
    • Apply appropriate signs in the formula
    • Calculate total inductance

  • 不考慮互感：

    • 直接相加各電感值
    • 範例：12H + 4H + 6H = 22H

  • 考慮互感：

    • 根據磁力線方向判斷互助或互消
    • 代入公式計算
    • 得出總電感值

Series Combination of Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 電感器串聯組合：有互感與無互感

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in series inductor combinations, covering both cases with and without mutual inductance. It includes formulas and practical considerations for different scenarios.

本文解釋電感器串聯組合的計算方法，包括有互感與無互感的情況。內容包含不同情況下的計算公式和實際考量。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Inductors in Series Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的串聯電感

  • The total inductance is the sum of the individual inductances:
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2$$

  • This is the basic formula for series inductors without mutual effects

  • 總電感為各電感直接相加：
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2$$

  • 這是不考慮互感時串聯電感的基本公式

2. Inductors in Series With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的串聯電感

  • Need to determine if mutual effect is:

    • Aiding (same magnetic field direction)
    • Opposing (opposite magnetic field direction)

  • The mutual inductance term affects the total inductance calculation

  • 需判斷互感是：

    • 互助（磁場方向相同）
    • 互消（磁場方向相反）

  • 互感項會影響總電感的計算

3. Series Aiding Configuration 串聯互助配置

  • The magnetic fields have the same direction

  • Mutual inductance is positive

  • Total inductance formula:
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M$$

  • 磁場方向相同

  • 互感為正值

  • 總電感公式：
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M$$

4. Series Opposing Configuration 串聯互消配置

  • The magnetic fields have opposite directions

  • Mutual inductance is negative

  • Total inductance formula:
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 - 2M$$

  • 磁場方向相反

  • 互感為負值

  • 總電感公式：
    $$L_{total} = L_1 + L_2 - 2M$$

5. Polarity Marking (N-dot Convention) 極性標記（N點標示法）

  • Use dots to indicate the polarity (N-pole direction) of the coils

  • Helps determine whether mutual inductance is aiding or opposing

  • Important for correct calculation of total inductance

  • 使用點來標示線圈的極性（N極方向）

  • 有助於判斷互感是互助或互消

  • 對正確計算總電感很重要

Magnetic Field Intensity of a Solenoid - 螺線管的磁場強度

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental concepts of magnetic field intensity in solenoids, including the calculation of magnetomotive force and the principles of magnetic field superposition.

本文解釋螺線管中磁場強度的基本概念，包括磁動勢的計算和磁場疊加原理。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Magnetomotive Force (MMF) 磁動勢

  • Definition:

    • Energy of magnetic flux generated by current
    • Formula: MMF = ( NI )
    • Where N is number of turns, I is current

  • 定義：

    • 電流產生的磁力線能量
    • 公式：磁動勢 = ( NI )
    • N為匝數，I為電流

2. Magnetic Field Intensity 磁場強度

  • Calculation Formula:
    $$
    H = \frac{NI}{L}
    $$

    • H: magnetic field intensity
    • N: number of turns
    • I: current
    • L: magnetic path length

  • 計算公式：
    $$
    H = \frac{NI}{L}
    $$

    • H：磁場強度
    • N：匝數
    • I：電流
    • L：磁路長度

3. Units 單位

  • Standard Unit:

    • Ampere-turns per meter (A·t/m)
    • Represents magnetic field strength
    • International standard unit

  • 標準單位：

    • 安匝/公尺（A·t/m）
    • 表示磁場強度
    • 國際標準單位

4. Field Superposition 磁場疊加

  • Principle:

    • Multiple coils’ fields can be added
    • Vector addition for same direction
    • Vector subtraction for opposite directions

  • 原理：

    • 多個線圈的磁場可以相加
    • 同向時做向量加法
    • 反向時做向量減法

5. Practical Applications 實際應用

  • Electromagnetic Devices:

    • Solenoids
    • Electromagnets
    • Transformers
    • Magnetic sensors

  • 電磁裝置：

    • 螺線管
    • 電磁鐵
    • 變壓器
    • 磁感測器

6. Important Considerations 重要考量

  • Factors Affecting Field Strength:

    • Number of turns
    • Current magnitude
    • Core material
    • Path length

  • 影響磁場強度的因素：

    • 匝數
    • 電流大小
    • 心材材料
    • 路徑長度

Conversion Between Thevenin and Norton Equivalent Circuits - 戴維寧等效電路與諾頓等效電路的互換

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-17 In Physics , Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the conversion between Thevenin and Norton equivalent circuits, including the mathematical formulas and practical applications in circuit analysis.

本文解釋戴維寧與諾頓等效電路之間的轉換，包括數學公式和電路分析中的實際應用。

Bilingual Display Controls 雙語顯示控制

  • [Show English Only]
  • [只顯示中文]
  • [Show Both Languages 同時顯示]

Key Points 重點歸納

English

  • Basic Concepts

    • Complex linear circuits can be simplified
    • Two equivalent circuit forms:
      • Thevenin equivalent
      • Norton equivalent
    • Both represent same circuit behavior

  • Thevenin to Norton Conversion

    • Norton current formula:
      $$
      I_N = \frac{E_{th}}{R_{th}}
      $$
    • Norton resistance:
      $$
      R_N = R_{th}
      $$
    • Maintains circuit equivalence

  • Norton to Thevenin Conversion

    • Thevenin voltage formula:
      $$
      E_{th} = I_N \times R_N
      $$
    • Thevenin resistance:
      $$
      R_{th} = R_N
      $$
    • Preserves circuit characteristics

  • Important Considerations

    • Voltage polarity must match current direction
    • Current flows from positive voltage terminal
    • Equivalent resistance remains unchanged
    • Power calculations give same results

  • Analysis Steps

    • For equivalent voltage:
      • Calculate open-circuit voltage
      • Remove load for measurement
    • For equivalent resistance:
      • Short voltage sources
      • Remove current sources
      • Calculate total resistance

中文

  • 基本概念

    • 複雜線性電路可以簡化
    • 兩種等效電路形式：
      • 戴維寧等效
      • 諾頓等效
    • 兩者表示相同電路行為

  • 戴維寧轉諾頓

    • 諾頓電流公式：
      $$
      I_N = \frac{E_{th}}{R_{th}}
      $$
    • 諾頓電阻：
      $$
      R_N = R_{th}
      $$
    • 保持電路等效性

  • 諾頓轉戴維寧

    • 戴維寧電壓公式：
      $$
      E_{th} = I_N \times R_N
      $$
    • 戴維寧電阻：
      $$
      R_{th} = R_N
      $$
    • 保持電路特性

  • 重要注意事項

    • 電壓極性需對應電流方向
    • 電流從電壓正端流出
    • 等效電阻保持不變
    • 功率計算結果相同

  • 分析步驟

    • 求等效電壓：
      • 計算開路電壓
