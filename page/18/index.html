2-4.4 Joule’s Law and Electric Current Heat Effect

Key Points

  • Discoverer of Joule’s Law: British physicist James Joule (1841)
  • Electric Current Heat Effect: Heat generated by collisions of charged particles when current passes through a resistor
  • Joule’s Law Formula:
    $$ H = I^2 \times R \times t $$
    where
    • (H): Heat energy (Joule, J)
    • (I): Current (Ampere, A)
    • (R): Resistance (Ohm, Ω)
    • (t): Time (seconds, s)
  • Heat Unit Conversion:
    • 1 calorie (cal) = 4.185 Joules (J)
    • 1 Joule (J) = 0.24 calories (cal)
  • Imperial Heat Unit: 1 BTU = 252 cal = 1055 J
  • Application of Heat Calculation: Use Joule’s law to calculate heat produced by resistors, convertible to cal or BTU
  • Heat and Temperature Change Relation:
    $$ H = m \times s \times \Delta T $$
    where
    • (m): Mass (grams, g)
    • (s): Specific heat (cal/g°C)
    • (\Delta T): Temperature change (°C)

2-4.4 焦耳定律與電流熱效應重點歸納

重點歸納

  • 焦耳定律發現者：英國物理學家詹姆斯·焦耳（1841年）
  • 電流熱效應：電流通過電阻器時，帶電粒子碰撞產生熱能
  • 焦耳定律公式：
    $$ H = I^2 \times R \times t $$
    其中，
    • (H)：熱量（焦耳，J）
    • (I)：電流（安培，A）
    • (R)：電阻（歐姆，Ω）
    • (t)：時間（秒，s）
  • 熱量單位轉換：
    • 1卡（cal）= 4.185焦耳（J）
    • 1焦耳（J）= 0.24卡（cal）
  • 英制熱量單位：1 BTU = 252卡 = 1055焦耳
  • 熱量計算應用：利用焦耳定律計算電阻器產生的熱量，並可轉換成卡或BTU
  • 熱量與溫度變化關係：
    $$ H = m \times s \times \Delta T $$
    其中，
    • (m)：質量（克，g）
    • (s)：比熱（cal/g°C）
    • (\Delta T)：溫度變化（°C）

Kirchhoff’s Current Law (3-7)

Key Points

  • Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL): At any node in an electrical circuit, the total current entering equals the total current leaving
  • Formula Representation:
    [ \sum I_{\text{in}} = \sum I_{\text{out}} ]
    or algebraic sum equals zero:
    [ \sum I = 0 ]
  • Node Current Conservation: The algebraic sum of currents at a node (inflows positive, outflows negative) is zero
  • Current Calculation: Use examples to calculate currents and find unknown values
  • Parallel Circuit Properties: In parallel circuits, node currents conserve and voltages are equal
  • Practical Applications: Examples include calculating currents and resistances to verify KCL

克希荷夫電流定律（3-7）

重點歸納

  • 克希荷夫電流定律 (KCL)：在電路中任一節點，流入的總電流等於流出的總電流
  • 公式表示：
    [ \sum I_{\text{流入}} = \sum I_{\text{流出}} ]
    或代數和為零：
    [ \sum I = 0 ]
  • 節點電流守恆：節點電流的代數和（流入為正，流出為負）等於零
  • 電流計算：透過實例計算節點電流，求出未知電流值
  • 並聯電路特性：並聯電路中，節點電流守恆，電壓相等
  • 實際應用：實例中計算電流和電阻，驗證KCL定律

2-5.5 Lenz’s Law and Electromagnetic Induction

Key Points

Lenz’s Law Fundamentals

  • Induced current creates a magnetic field that opposes the change in original magnetic flux
  • Complements Faraday’s Law by explaining EMF polarity
  • Essential for understanding electromagnetic induction phenomena

N Pole Approaching the Coil

  • Induced current creates magnetic field opposing N pole approach
  • Magnetic field direction: right to left
  • Current flows from B to A
  • EMF polarity: A positive, B negative

N Pole Moving Away from the Coil

  • Coil end forms S pole to attract N pole
  • Magnetic field direction: to the right
  • Current flows from A to B
  • EMF polarity: B positive, A negative

Practical Applications

  • Use Ampere’s Right-Hand Rule for current direction
  • Understanding through “chasing girlfriend” analogy
  • Important for electromagnetic device design

2-5.5 楞次定律與電磁感應

重點歸納

楞次定律基礎

  • 感應電流產生磁場，反抗原有磁通的變化
  • 補充法拉第定律，說明電動勢極性
  • 理解電磁感應現象的關鍵

N極靠近線圈

  • 感應電流產生磁場反抗N極接近
  • 磁場方向：由右到左
  • 電流方向：由B流入A流出
  • 電動勢極性：A正B負

N極遠離線圈

  • 線圈端產生S極吸引N極
  • 磁場方向：向右
  • 電流方向：由A流入B流出
  • 電動勢極性：B正A負

實際應用

  • 使用安培右手定則判斷電流方向
  • 透過「追女朋友」比喻理解
  • 電磁裝置設計的重要依據

2-4.2 Ohm’s Law Summary

Key Points

  • Ohm’s Law was discovered by German physicist Georg Ohm in 1862, describing the relationship between voltage (V), current (I), and resistance (R) across a resistor.
  • Applying voltage ( V ) across a resistor causes current ( I ) to flow.
  • When resistance ( R ) is constant, increasing voltage ( V ) increases current ( I ).
  • When voltage ( V ) is constant, decreasing resistance ( R ) increases current ( I ).
  • Mathematical expressions:
    • ( I = \frac{V}{R} ) (Current equals voltage divided by resistance)
    • ( R = \frac{V}{I} ) (Resistance equals voltage divided by current)
    • ( V = I \times R ) (Voltage equals current times resistance)
  • Units:
    • Voltage ( V ): Volt (V)
    • Current ( I ): Ampere (A)
    • Resistance ( R ): Ohm (Ω)
  • Voltage and current are proportional; the proportionality constant is the resistance.
  • The voltage-current graph is a straight line with slope equal to the resistance.

2-4.2 歐姆定律重點歸納

重點歸納

  • 歐姆定律由德國物理學家奧爾格·歐姆於1862年發現，描述電阻器上的電壓 (V)、電流 (I) 與電阻值 (R) 之間的關係。
  • 電阻器兩端加電壓 ( V )，會產生電流 ( I )。
  • 在電阻值 ( R ) 不變時，電壓 ( V ) 增大，電流 ( I ) 也會增大。
  • 在電壓 ( V ) 不變時，電阻 ( R ) 減小，電流 ( I ) 會增大。
  • 數學表達式：
    • ( I = \frac{V}{R} ) （電流等於電壓除以電阻）
    • ( R = \frac{V}{I} ) （電阻等於電壓除以電流）
    • ( V = I \times R ) （電壓等於電流乘以電阻）
  • 單位：
    • 電壓 ( V )：伏特 (Volt, V)
    • 電流 ( I )：安培 (Ampere, A)
    • 電阻 ( R )：歐姆 (Ohm, Ω)
  • 電壓與電流成正比，比例常數即為電阻值。
  • 電壓-電流關係圖為一條斜率為電阻值的直線。

Application Examples of Parallel Circuits (3-9)

Key Points

  • Ammeter Structure: Composed of a galvanometer and internal resistance RA in series; the lower the internal resistance, the better, ideally zero
  • Current Measurement: The ammeter must be connected in series with the component being measured
  • Multiplier (Shunt Resistor): To extend the ammeter’s measuring range, a low-resistance resistor RS is connected in parallel, called a shunt resistor
  • Multiplier Formula:
    [ n = \frac{I_T}{I_A} = 1 + \frac{R_A}{R_S} ]
  • Shunt Resistor Calculation:
    [ R_S = \frac{R_A}{n - 1} ]
  • Examples:
    • Ammeter full scale 10mA, internal resistance 50Ω, to measure 1A, parallel a 0.5Ω shunt resistor
    • If RS is 1/9 of RA, the measuring range is extended 10 times
  • Parallel Appliances Rated Voltage Judgment:
    • Appliances with matching rated voltage work normally (e.g., computer, refrigerator at 110V)
    • Appliances with rated voltage lower than supply voltage will be damaged (e.g., 24V intercom connected to 110V)
    • Appliances with rated voltage higher than supply voltage cannot operate properly (e.g., 220V air conditioner connected to 110V)

並聯電路應用實例（3-9）

重點歸納

  • 電流表結構：由檢流計與內電阻RA串聯組成，內電阻越低越好，理想為零
  • 測量電流：電流表必須與被測元件串聯
  • 倍增器（分流器）：為擴大電流表測量範圍，並聯一個低阻值電阻RS，稱為分流器
  • 倍增率公式：
    [ n = \frac{I_T}{I_A} = 1 + \frac{R_A}{R_S} ]
  • 分流器電阻計算：
    [ R_S = \frac{R_A}{n - 1} ]
  • 實例：
    • 電流表滿刻度10mA，內阻50Ω，要測量1A，需並聯0.5Ω分流器
    • 若RS是RA的1/9，測量範圍擴大為10倍
  • 並聯電器額定電壓判斷：
    • 額定電壓相符可正常工作（如電腦、冰箱110V）
    • 額定電壓低於供電電壓會燒毀（如24V對講機接110V）
    • 額定電壓高於供電電壓無法正常運作（如220V冷氣接110V）
