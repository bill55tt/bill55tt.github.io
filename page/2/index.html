Introduction to Coulomb's Magnetic Force Law and Magnetic Field Intensity - 庫侖磁力定律與磁場強度介紹

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article explains Coulomb’s magnetic force law and its relationship with magnetic field intensity, including the mathematical formulas and practical applications in electromagnetism.

Key Points

  • Coulomb’s magnetic force law is analogous to Newton’s law of universal gravitation

  • Magnetic force magnitude is proportional to the product of magnetic pole strengths

  • Force is inversely proportional to the square of the distance

  • Magnetic force formula:
    $$
    F = K \frac{M_1 M_2}{d^2}
    $$
    where:

    • F: magnetic force
    • M₁, M₂: magnetic pole strengths
    • d: distance between poles
    • K: magnetic force constant

  • Magnetic pole strength unit is Weber (Wb)

  • Magnetic force constant in air/vacuum: K = 6.33 × 10⁴ (includes Henry)

  • Magnetic field intensity (H) is force per unit magnetic pole:
    $$
    H = \frac{F}{m}
    $$
    where:

    • F: force
    • m: magnetic pole strength

  • Magnetic field intensity is a vector quantity

  • Direction follows magnetic field line tangent

引言

本文解釋庫侖磁力定律及其與磁場強度的關係，包括數學公式和電磁學中的實際應用。

重點歸納

  • 庫侖磁力定律類似牛頓萬有引力定律

  • 磁力大小與磁極強度成正比

  • 磁力與距離平方成反比

  • 磁力公式：
    $$
    F = K \frac{M_1 M_2}{d^2}
    $$
    其中：

    • F：磁力
    • M₁, M₂：磁極強度
    • d：磁極間距離
    • K：磁力常數

  • 磁極強度單位為韋伯（Wb）

  • 空氣或真空中磁力常數：K = 6.33 × 10⁴（含亨利）

  • 磁場強度（H）定義為單位磁極受力：
    $$
    H = \frac{F}{m}
    $$
    其中：

    • F：受力
    • m：磁極強度

  • 磁場強度為向量

  • 方向為磁力線切線方向

Analysis of Coupling Coefficient, Self-Inductance, and Mutual Inductance Relationships - 耦合係數、自感與互感關係式解析

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article analyzes the relationships between coupling coefficient, self-inductance, and mutual inductance in electromagnetic systems. It explains the fundamental concepts and mathematical relationships that govern these interactions.

本文分析電磁系統中耦合係數、自感和互感之間的關係。內容解釋這些相互作用的基本概念和數學關係。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Coupling Coefficient 耦合係數

  • Coupling coefficient K:

    • Represents the magnetic coupling efficiency between two coils
    • Ranges from 0 to 1
    • Closer to 1 indicates better coupling

  • 耦合係數K：

    • 表示兩線圈間磁通的導磁效率
    • 範圍為0到1
    • 越接近1表示耦合越好

2. Coupling Efficiencies 耦合效率

  • K1 and K2:

    • K1: Magnetic coupling efficiency from coil 1 to coil 2
    • K2: Magnetic coupling efficiency from coil 2 to coil 1
    • K1 = K2 in practice

  • K1與K2：

    • K1：線圈1對線圈2磁通的導磁效率
    • K2：線圈2對線圈1的導磁效率
    • 實際應用中K1 = K2

3. Magnetic Flux 磁通量

  • Magnetic flux relationships:

    • Φ12: Magnetic flux from coil 1 affecting coil 2
    • Φ21: Magnetic flux from coil 2 affecting coil 1

  • 磁通關係：

    • Φ12：線圈1產生影響線圈2的磁通
    • Φ21：線圈2影響線圈1的磁通

4. Self-Inductance 自感量

  • Self-inductance values:

    • L1: Inductance of coil 1
    • L2: Inductance of coil 2

  • 自感量：

    • L1：線圈1的電感量
    • L2：線圈2的電感量

5. Mutual Inductance 互感量

  • Mutual inductance formula:
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • Equality of mutual inductances:
    [
    M_{12} = M_{21} = M
    ]

  • 互感量公式：
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • 互感量相等關係：
    [
    M_{12} = M_{21} = M
    ]

Direction of Force on Current-Carrying Conductor in Magnetic Field and Fleming's Left-Hand Rule - 載流導體在磁場中所受力的方向與佛萊明左手定則

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the principles of force direction on current-carrying conductors in magnetic fields and the application of Fleming’s left-hand rule, including magnetic field interactions and practical applications.

本文解釋載流導體在磁場中受力方向的原理和佛萊明左手定則的應用，包括磁場相互作用和實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Current-Carrying Conductor and Magnetic Field 載流導體與磁場

  • Current-Carrying Conductor: A conductor with electric current generates a magnetic field.

  • Main Magnetic Field Direction: Magnetic lines run from the North (N) pole to the South (S) pole.

  • 載流導體：導體中通有電流，會產生磁場。

  • 主極磁場方向：磁力線由N極指向S極。

2. Magnetic Field Interactions 磁場相互作用

  • Interaction of Magnetic Lines:

    • Above the conductor, magnetic lines oppose and weaken each other.
    • Below the conductor, magnetic lines align and strengthen.

  • Force Direction: The conductor is pushed from regions of higher magnetic line density to lower density, resulting in an upward force.

  • 磁力線互動：

    • 導體上方磁力線方向相反，磁力線抵銷，磁場減弱。
    • 導體下方磁力線方向相同，磁力線增強。

  • 受力方向：導體受力由磁力線密度高處推向低處，導體向上受力。

3. Fleming’s Left-Hand Rule 佛萊明左手定則

  • Fleming’s Left-Hand Rule:

    • Thumb: Force direction (F)
    • Index finger: Magnetic field direction (B)
    • Middle finger: Current direction (I)
    • All three are perpendicular; use the left hand to determine the force direction on the conductor.

  • Application: Fleming’s left-hand rule simplifies determining the force direction on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field.

  • 佛萊明左手定則：

    • 拇指：受力方向（F）
    • 食指：磁場方向（B）
    • 中指：電流方向（I）
    • 三者互相垂直，用左手判斷載流導體受力方向。

  • 應用：佛萊明左手定則簡化判斷載流導體在磁場中受力方向的過程。

Magnitude of Force on a Current-Carrying Conductor in a Magnetic Field - 載流導體在磁場中所受力的大小

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article examines the calculation of force magnitude on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field, including both perpendicular and angled cases, along with practical examples.

本文探討載流導體在磁場中受力大小的計算方法，包括垂直和斜角兩種情況，並提供實際範例。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Force Calculation Methods 受力計算方法

  • The force magnitude on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field is calculated in two cases:

    1. Conductor perpendicular to magnetic field:
      Formula: ( F = B l I )
      where ( F ) is force (Newtons), ( B ) is magnetic flux density (Tesla), ( l ) is effective length of conductor in the field (meters), and ( I ) is current (Amperes).
    2. Conductor at an angle (\theta) to magnetic field:
      Formula: ( F = B l I \sin \theta ), where (\sin \theta) adjusts the effective length cut by the magnetic field.

  • 載流導體在磁場中受力的大小計算分兩種情況：

    1. 導體與磁場垂直：
      公式為 ( F = B l I )
      其中，
      • ( F ) 是受力大小（牛頓，N）
      • ( B ) 是磁通密度（特斯拉，T）
      • ( l ) 是導體在磁場中的有效長度（公尺，m）
      • ( I ) 是電流大小（安培，A）
    2. 導體與磁場不垂直（與磁場成角度 (\theta)）：
      公式修正為 ( F = B l I \sin \theta )，其中 (\sin \theta) 是導體在磁場中切割的有效長度比例。

2. Force Direction 力的方向

  • The force direction is determined by Fleming’s left-hand rule:
    Thumb points to force, index finger to magnetic field, middle finger to current.

  • 力的方向可用佛萊明左手定則判斷：
    拇指指向力的方向，食指指向磁場方向，中指指向電流方向。

3. Example Calculations 範例計算

  • Example calculations:

    • Perpendicular case: ( B=2,T, l=1,m, I=10,A \Rightarrow F=20,N )
    • Angled case: ( B=0.5,T, l=0.2,m, I=10,A, \theta=30^\circ \Rightarrow F=0.5,N )

  • 範例計算：

    • 垂直情況：( B=2,T, l=1,m, I=10,A \Rightarrow F=20,N )
    • 斜角情況：( B=0.5,T, l=0.2,m, I=10,A, \theta=30^\circ \Rightarrow F=0.5,N )

Current Magnetic Effect: Oersted's Experiment and Applications - 電流的磁效應：奧斯特實驗與應用

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains Oersted’s discovery of the magnetic effect of electric current and its significance in understanding the relationship between electricity and magnetism.

本文解釋厄斯特發現電流磁效應的過程，以及這個發現對理解電與磁關係的重要性。

Key Points 重點歸納

English

  • Oersted’s groundbreaking discovery:

    • Electric current produces a magnetic field
    • Magnetic field affects compass needle orientation
    • First demonstration of electricity-magnetism relationship

  • Current direction effects:

    • Changing current direction reverses compass deflection
    • Demonstrates vector nature of magnetic field
    • Shows field-current relationship

  • Magnetic field characteristics:

    • Field strength proportional to current magnitude
    • Field strength inversely proportional to distance
    • Field direction perpendicular to current flow

  • Ampere’s right-hand rule:

    • Thumb points in current direction
    • Fingers curl in magnetic field direction
    • Practical tool for field direction determination

  • Historical significance:

    • Oersted’s discovery (1820)
    • Foundation for electromagnetism
    • Ampere’s theoretical development

中文

  • 厄斯特的重大發現：

    • 電流會產生磁場
    • 磁場會影響磁針方向
    • 首次證明電與磁的關係

  • 電流方向效應：

    • 改變電流方向會反轉磁針偏轉
    • 顯示磁場的向量特性
    • 展示場與電流的關係

  • 磁場特性：

    • 磁場強度與電流大小成正比
    • 磁場強度與距離成反比
    • 磁場方向與電流方向垂直

  • 安培右手定則：

    • 拇指指向電流方向
    • 手指彎曲方向為磁場方向
    • 實用的磁場方向判斷工具

  • 歷史意義：

    • 厄斯特的發現（1820年）
    • 電磁學的基礎
    • 安培的理論發展
