Example of Force Between Two Long Straight Conductors Carrying Current - 兩長直導體通電流所受之力範例解析

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article examines the magnetic force between two long straight conductors carrying current, including the calculation formula and practical examples with different scenarios.

本文探討兩長直導體通電流時彼此間的磁力作用，包括計算公式和不同情況下的實際範例。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Principles 基本原理

  • Two long straight conductors carrying current exert magnetic forces on each other.

  • Force magnitude formula:
    $$
    F = \frac{\mu l I_1 I_2}{2 \pi d}
    $$
    where $\mu$ is permeability, $l$ is length, $I_1, I_2$ are currents, and $d$ is distance between conductors.

  • 兩長直導體通電流時，彼此會產生磁力作用。

  • 受力大小公式為：
    $$
    F = \frac{\mu l I_1 I_2}{2 \pi d}
    $$
    其中，$\mu$為磁導率，$l$為導體長度，$I_1, I_2$為電流，$d$為兩導體間距。

2. Force Direction 力的方向

  • Same current direction causes attraction; opposite direction causes repulsion.

  • The direction of force can be determined using the right-hand rule for magnetic fields.

  • 電流方向相同時，導體間力為相吸；方向相反時，力為相斥。

  • 力的方向可用磁場的右手定則判斷。

3. Example Calculations 範例計算

  • Example 1: $I_1=10A$, $I_2=20A$, $l=10m$, $d=1m$, force is $4 \times 10^{-4} N$, attractive.

  • Example 2: Two wires 0.5m apart, current 20A, length 50m, force is 0.008N, repulsive.

  • 範例1：$I_1=10A$, $I_2=20A$, $l=10m$, $d=1m$，計算出力為 $4 \times 10^{-4} N$，方向相吸。

  • 範例2：電路中兩條線距離0.5m，電流20A，長度50m，計算力為0.008N，方向相斥。

High School Basic Electricity: Inductance Calculations Part B - 高中基本電學：電感量計算 PART B

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article presents Part B of the inductance calculations series, focusing on basic inductance calculations with practical examples. It demonstrates how to calculate inductance using different formulas and parameters.

本文介紹電感量計算系列的B部分，重點在於基本電感計算的實際範例。內容展示如何使用不同的公式和參數來計算電感量。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Example 1: Basic Inductance Calculation 範例一：基本電感量計算

  • Given parameters:

    • Number of turns (N) = 100
    • Current (I) = 10 A
    • Magnetic flux (Φ) = 0.2 Weber

  • Formula:
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]

  • Calculation:
    [
    L = \frac{100 \times 0.2}{10} = 2 \text{ Henry}
    ]

  • 給定參數：

    • 匝數(N) = 100
    • 電流(I) = 10安培
    • 磁通量(Φ) = 0.2韋伯

  • 公式：
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]

  • 計算：
    [
    L = \frac{100 \times 0.2}{10} = 2 \text{ 亨利}
    ]

2. Example 2: Inductance with Core 範例二：含鐵心電感量計算

  • Given parameters:

    • Cross-sectional area (A) = (10^{-2} m^2)
    • Length (l) = (10^{-1} m)
    • Permeability (μ) = (10^{-4} H/m)
    • Number of turns (N) = 1000

  • Formula:
    [
    L = \frac{\mu A N^2}{l}
    ]

  • Calculation:
    [
    L = \frac{10^{-4} \times 10^{-2} \times 1000^2}{10^{-1}} = 10 \text{ Henry}
    ]

  • Effect of halving turns:

    • New inductance = 2.5 Henry (1/4 of original)

  • 給定參數：

    • 截面積(A) = (10^{-2} m^2)
    • 長度(l) = (10^{-1} m)
    • 導磁係數(μ) = (10^{-4} H/m)
    • 匝數(N) = 1000

  • 公式：
    [
    L = \frac{\mu A N^2}{l}
    ]

  • 計算：
    [
    L = \frac{10^{-4} \times 10^{-2} \times 1000^2}{10^{-1}} = 10 \text{ 亨利}
    ]

  • 匝數減半的影響：

    • 新電感量 = 2.5亨利（原來的1/4）

3. Key Observations 重要觀察

  • Inductance is proportional to the square of the number of turns

  • Changes in the number of turns have a significant impact on inductance

  • Core material properties (permeability) affect inductance

  • 電感量與匝數的平方成正比

  • 匝數的變化對電感量有顯著影響

  • 鐵心材料特性（導磁係數）影響電感量

Introduction 引言

This article presents Part E of the inductance calculations series, focusing on basic calculations of self-inductance (L) and mutual inductance (M), as well as comprehensive calculations involving inductance (L), coupling coefficient (K), and mutual inductance (M).

本文介紹電感量計算系列的第五部分，重點在於自感量(L)和互感量(M)的基本計算，以及包含電感量(L)、耦合係數(K)和互感量(M)的綜合計算。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Parameters 基本參數

  • Relationship between coil turns (N_1, N_2), currents (I_1, I_2), and magnetic flux (\Phi)

  • These parameters form the foundation of inductance calculations

  • 線圈匝數 (N_1, N_2)、電流 (I_1, I_2) 與磁通量 (\Phi) 的關係

  • 這些參數構成電感計算的基礎

2. Self-Inductance Formula 自感量公式

  • Self-inductance calculation:
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]

  • This formula shows the relationship between turns, flux, and current

  • 自感量計算公式：
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]

  • 此公式顯示匝數、磁通量和電流之間的關係

3. Mutual Inductance Formulas 互感量公式

  • Mutual inductance calculations:
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}, \quad M_{21} = \frac{N_1 \Phi_{21}}{I_2}
    ]

  • These formulas describe the mutual inductance between two coils

  • 互感量計算公式：
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}, \quad M_{21} = \frac{N_1 \Phi_{21}}{I_2}
    ]

  • 這些公式描述兩個線圈之間的互感

4. Coupling Coefficient 耦合係數

  • Coupling coefficient formula:
    [
    K = \frac{\Phi_{12}}{\Phi_1} = \frac{\Phi_{21}}{\Phi_2}
    ]

  • This coefficient measures the strength of magnetic coupling

  • 耦合係數計算：
    [
    K = \frac{\Phi_{12}}{\Phi_1} = \frac{\Phi_{21}}{\Phi_2}
    ]

  • 此係數衡量磁耦合的強度

5. Self-Inductance Ratio 自感量比值

  • Ratio of self-inductances:
    [
    \frac{L_1}{L_2} = \left(\frac{N_1}{N_2}\right)^2
    ]

  • This relationship is useful for comparing inductances

  • 自感量比值：
    [
    \frac{L_1}{L_2} = \left(\frac{N_1}{N_2}\right)^2
    ]

  • 此關係可用於比較電感量

Energy Storage in Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 電感器的儲能（無互感與有互感情況）

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article provides a comprehensive explanation of energy storage in inductors, covering both cases with and without mutual inductance. It includes detailed formulas and practical applications.

本文全面解釋電感器的儲能原理，包括有互感與無互感的情況。內容包含詳細公式和實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Energy Storage 基本儲能原理

  • When current flows through an inductor, it creates a magnetic field that stores magnetic energy

  • Magnetic flux linkage (λ) is proportional to current (I), with inductance L as the proportionality constant

  • Stored energy is the area under the λ-I curve

  • 電感器通電後產生磁場並儲存磁能

  • 磁通鏈（λ）與電流（I）成正比，比例常數為電感量L

  • 儲存能量為磁通鏈與電流的面積

2. Energy Storage Formula 儲能計算公式

  • Basic formula for single inductor:
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2
    $$

  • This formula represents the energy stored in the magnetic field

  • 單一電感器基本公式：
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2
    $$

  • 此公式代表儲存在磁場中的能量

3. Mutual Inductance Energy Storage 互感儲能計算

  • With mutual inductance, total stored energy includes self and mutual inductance terms:
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    $$

  • The plus sign indicates aiding mutual inductance, the minus sign indicates opposing

  • 有互感時，總儲能包含自感與互感能量：
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    $$

  • 其中正號代表互助，負號代表互消

4. Series and Parallel Configurations 串聯與並聯配置

  • In series aiding or opposing cases:

    • Currents are equal
    • Formula can be simplified
    • Total inductance includes mutual inductance term

  • In parallel aiding or opposing cases:

    • Currents differ
    • Must be calculated separately
    • Consider both self and mutual inductance effects

  • 串聯互助與互消情況：

    • 電流相等
    • 公式可簡化
    • 總電感包含互感項

  • 並聯互助與互消情況：

    • 電流不等
    • 需分別計算
    • 考慮自感與互感效應

Energy Storage Calculation for Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 不考慮互感的電感器儲能計算與考慮互感的電感器儲能計算範例

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating energy storage in inductors, both with and without considering mutual inductance effects. It includes practical examples and formulas for different scenarios.

本文解釋電感器儲能計算的方法，包括考慮和不考慮互感效應的情況。內容包含實際範例和不同情況下的計算公式。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Energy Storage Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的儲能計算

  • Use the formula ( W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2 )

  • Example: A 10mH inductor with 6A current stores 0.18J energy

  • This is the basic formula for single inductor energy storage

  • 使用公式 ( W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2 )

  • 範例：10mH電感器通過6A電流，計算儲能為0.18J

  • 這是單一電感器儲能的基本公式

2. Energy Storage With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的儲能計算

  • For two coils, total energy is:
    [
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    ]

  • The sign depends on whether the mutual inductance is aiding (+) or opposing (−)

  • 兩線圈儲能為：
    [
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    ]

  • 其中符號由互感是互助（正號）或互消（負號）決定

3. Mutual Inductance Calculation 互感計算

  • Mutual inductance can be calculated using:
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • where (K) is the coupling coefficient

  • 互感計算公式：
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • 其中 (K) 是耦合係數

4. Series Aiding Inductors 串聯互助電感

  • For series aiding inductors:

    1. Calculate total inductance:
      [
      L_{\text{total}} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M
      ]
    2. Then calculate energy:
      [
      W = \frac{1}{2} L_{\text{total}} I^2
      ]

  • 串聯互助電感總儲能：

    1. 先計算總電感：
      [
      L_{\text{total}} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M
      ]
    2. 再用公式計算儲能：
      [
      W = \frac{1}{2} L_{\text{total}} I^2
      ]

Introduction to the Structure and Function of Inductors - 電感器的結構與功能介紹

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article provides a comprehensive overview of inductors, their structure, types, and working principles. It explains how inductors store energy and their role in electrical circuits.

本文全面介紹電感器的結構、種類和工作原理。解釋電感器如何儲存能量及其在電路中的作用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Definition 基本定義

  • Symbol and Unit:

    • Symbol: L
    • Unit: Henry (H)
    • Named after Joseph Henry, who discovered inductance

  • 符號與單位：

    • 符號：L
    • 單位：亨利（H）
    • 紀念發現電感特性的科學家約瑟夫·亨利

2. Structure 結構

  • Core Components:

    • Conductor wire wound around a core
    • Core materials: air, iron, or magnetic material
    • Core material affects inductance value

  • 核心組件：

    • 導線繞在心材上
    • 心材可為空氣、鐵心或磁心
    • 心材影響電感量大小

3. Function 功能

  • Main Functions:

    • Converts electrical energy to magnetic energy
    • Stores energy in magnetic field
    • Stabilizes current flow
    • Prevents sudden current changes
    • Reduces magnetic interference

  • 主要功能：

    • 將電能轉換成磁能
    • 在磁場中儲存能量
    • 穩定電流
    • 防止電流瞬間變化
    • 減少磁干擾

4. Types 種類

  • Classification by Core Material:

    • Air-core inductors
    • Iron-core inductors
    • Magnetic-core inductors
    • Printed circuit inductors

  • 依心材分類：

    • 空氣心電感器
    • 鐵心電感器
    • 磁心電感器
    • 印刷電路電感器

5. Working Principle 工作原理

  • Charging Process:

    • Current gradually increases
    • Magnetic energy stored in inductor
    • Magnetic field builds up

  • 充電過程：

    • 電流逐漸增加
    • 磁能儲存在電感器中
    • 磁場逐漸建立

6. Discharging Process 放電過程

  • Characteristics:

    • Magnetic energy converts to electrical energy
    • Current gradually decreases
    • Connected lamp brightness changes accordingly

  • 特性：

    • 磁能轉換成電能
    • 電流逐漸減小
    • 連接的燈泡亮度隨之變化

7. Important Considerations 重要注意事項

  • Switch Operation:

    • Switches must be toggled simultaneously
    • Prevents circuit open-circuiting
    • Avoids magnetic energy loss

  • 開關操作：

    • 開關必須同時切換
    • 防止電路瞬間開路

Magnetic Field Intensity of a Long Straight Conductor - 長直導線的磁場強度

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article introduces the concept and calculation of the magnetic field intensity around a long straight conductor, drawing analogies from the electric field of a parallel plate capacitor. It covers the formula, unit, direction, and practical examples.

本文介紹長直導線周圍磁場強度的概念與計算，並以平行板電容器的電場強度作類比推導，涵蓋公式、單位、方向與實例。

Key Points 重點歸納

English

  • The concept of electric field intensity in a parallel plate capacitor is used to analogously derive the magnetic field intensity of a long straight conductor.
  • In a parallel plate capacitor, electric field intensity equals voltage divided by the distance between plates.
  • The magnetic field intensity around a long straight conductor depends on the current (magnetomotive force) and the length of the magnetic field line (magnetic path length).
  • Magnetic field intensity formula:
    $$
    H = \frac{I}{2 \pi R}
    $$
    where (I) is the current in amperes and (R) is the radius (distance from the wire) in meters.
  • The unit of magnetic field intensity is ampere-turn per meter (AT/m).
  • The direction of the magnetic field follows the right-hand rule, with magnetic field lines forming circles around the conductor.
  • Example: For a 10-ampere current at 10 cm from the conductor, the magnetic field intensity is (\frac{50}{\pi} , \text{AT/m}), directed into the plane.

中文

  • 利用平行板電容器的電場強度概念，類比推導長直導線的磁場強度。
  • 平行板電容器中，電場強度為電壓除以兩極板距離。
  • 長直導線產生的磁場強度與電流（磁動勢）及磁力線長度（磁路長）有關。
  • 磁場強度公式：
    $$
    H = \frac{I}{2 \pi R}
    $$
    其中 (I) 是電流（安培），(R) 是距離導線的半徑（公尺）。
  • 磁場強度單位為安匝每公尺（AT/m）。
  • 磁場方向依安培右手定則判斷，磁力線繞導線形成圓周。
  • 範例：通入10安培電流，距離導線10公分處磁場強度為 (\frac{50}{\pi} , \text{AT/m})，方向射入紙面。

Key Points 重點歸納

English

  • Magnetic Flux Unit: Maxwell

    • Unit of magnetic lines of force
    • CGS unit of magnetic flux
    • Fundamental unit in electromagnetic calculations

  • Magnetic Flux (Φ, Phi)

    • Total number of magnetic lines of force
    • Measured in Weber (SI unit)
    • Conversion: 1 Weber = 10^8 Maxwell

  • Magnetic Flux Density (B)

    • Magnetic flux per unit area
    • Formula:
      $$
      B = \frac{\Phi}{A}
      $$
    • Units:
      • Tesla (SI)
      • Gauss (CGS)
      • Conversion: 1 Tesla = 10^4 Gauss

  • Unit Conversions

    • 1 Weber = 10^8 Maxwell
    • 1 Tesla = 10^4 Gauss
    • Essential for system conversion

  • Example Calculation

    • Area: 10 cm²
    • Flux: 10^6 Maxwell
    • Flux density: 10^5 Gauss
    • Converted to Tesla: 10 Tesla

中文

  • 磁力線單位：馬克士威

    • 磁力線的計量單位
    • CGS制的磁通量單位
    • 電磁計算的基本單位

  • 磁通量 (Φ, Phi)

    • 所有磁力線數量的總和
    • 單位為韋伯（SI制）
    • 換算：1韋伯 = 10^8 馬克士威

  • 磁通密度 (B)

    • 單位面積的磁通量
    • 公式：
      $$
      B = \frac{\Phi}{A}
      $$
    • 單位：
      • 忒斯拉（SI制）
      • 高斯（CGS制）
      • 換算：1忒斯拉 = 10^4 高斯

  • 單位換算

    • 1韋伯 = 10^8 馬克士威
