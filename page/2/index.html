High School Basic Electricity: Inductance Calculations - 高中基本電學：電感量計算範例

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental calculations of inductance, including self-inductance, mutual inductance, and coupling coefficient. It provides formulas and examples for various inductance calculations.

本文解釋電感的基本計算，包括自感量、互感量和耦合係數。內容提供各種電感計算的公式和範例。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Relationships 基本關係

  • Relationship between coil turns (N_1, N_2), currents (I_1, I_2), and magnetic flux (\Phi)

  • These parameters are fundamental to inductance calculations

  • 線圈匝數 (N_1, N_2)、電流 (I_1, I_2) 與磁通量 (\Phi) 的關係

  • 這些參數是電感計算的基礎

2. Self-Inductance Calculation 自感量計算

  • Self-inductance formula:
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]

  • This formula relates the number of turns, magnetic flux, and current

  • 自感量計算公式：
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]

  • 此公式關聯了匝數、磁通量和電流

3. Mutual Inductance Calculation 互感量計算

  • Mutual inductance formulas:
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}, \quad M_{21} = \frac{N_1 \Phi_{21}}{I_2}
    ]

  • These formulas calculate the mutual inductance between two coils

  • 互感量計算公式：
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}, \quad M_{21} = \frac{N_1 \Phi_{21}}{I_2}
    ]

  • 這些公式計算兩個線圈之間的互感

4. Coupling Coefficient 耦合係數

  • Coupling coefficient calculation:
    [
    K = \frac{\Phi_{12}}{\Phi_1} = \frac{\Phi_{21}}{\Phi_2}
    ]

  • This coefficient indicates the strength of magnetic coupling

  • 耦合係數計算：
    [
    K = \frac{\Phi_{12}}{\Phi_1} = \frac{\Phi_{21}}{\Phi_2}
    ]

  • 此係數表示磁耦合的強度

5. Self-Inductance Ratio 自感量比值

  • Using the square ratio of turns to find the ratio of self-inductances:
    [
    \frac{L_1}{L_2} = \left(\frac{N_1}{N_2}\right)^2
    ]

  • This relationship is useful for comparing inductances

  • 利用匝數平方比求自感量比值：
    [
    \frac{L_1}{L_2} = \left(\frac{N_1}{N_2}\right)^2
    ]

  • 此關係可用於比較電感量

6. Comprehensive Calculations 綜合運算

  • Demonstration of comprehensive calculations including:

    • Self-inductance
    • Mutual inductance
    • Coupling coefficient

  • These calculations combine multiple concepts

  • 綜合運算示範，包含：

    • 自感量
    • 互感量
    • 耦合係數

  • 這些計算結合了多個概念

Energy Storage in Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 電感器的儲能（無互感與有互感情況）

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article provides a comprehensive explanation of energy storage in inductors, covering both cases with and without mutual inductance. It includes detailed formulas and practical applications.

本文全面解釋電感器的儲能原理，包括有互感與無互感的情況。內容包含詳細公式和實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Energy Storage 基本儲能原理

  • When current flows through an inductor, it creates a magnetic field that stores magnetic energy

  • Magnetic flux linkage (λ) is proportional to current (I), with inductance L as the proportionality constant

  • Stored energy is the area under the λ-I curve

  • 電感器通電後產生磁場並儲存磁能

  • 磁通鏈（λ）與電流（I）成正比，比例常數為電感量L

  • 儲存能量為磁通鏈與電流的面積

2. Energy Storage Formula 儲能計算公式

  • Basic formula for single inductor:
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2
    $$

  • This formula represents the energy stored in the magnetic field

  • 單一電感器基本公式：
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2
    $$

  • 此公式代表儲存在磁場中的能量

3. Mutual Inductance Energy Storage 互感儲能計算

  • With mutual inductance, total stored energy includes self and mutual inductance terms:
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    $$

  • The plus sign indicates aiding mutual inductance, the minus sign indicates opposing

  • 有互感時，總儲能包含自感與互感能量：
    $$
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    $$

  • 其中正號代表互助，負號代表互消

4. Series and Parallel Configurations 串聯與並聯配置

  • In series aiding or opposing cases:

    • Currents are equal
    • Formula can be simplified
    • Total inductance includes mutual inductance term

  • In parallel aiding or opposing cases:

    • Currents differ
    • Must be calculated separately
    • Consider both self and mutual inductance effects

  • 串聯互助與互消情況：

    • 電流相等
    • 公式可簡化
    • 總電感包含互感項

  • 並聯互助與互消情況：

    • 電流不等
    • 需分別計算
    • 考慮自感與互感效應

Energy Storage Calculation for Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 不考慮互感的電感器儲能計算與考慮互感的電感器儲能計算範例

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating energy storage in inductors, both with and without considering mutual inductance effects. It includes practical examples and formulas for different scenarios.

本文解釋電感器儲能計算的方法，包括考慮和不考慮互感效應的情況。內容包含實際範例和不同情況下的計算公式。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Energy Storage Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的儲能計算

  • Use the formula ( W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2 )

  • Example: A 10mH inductor with 6A current stores 0.18J energy

  • This is the basic formula for single inductor energy storage

  • 使用公式 ( W = \frac{1}{2} L I^2 )

  • 範例：10mH電感器通過6A電流，計算儲能為0.18J

  • 這是單一電感器儲能的基本公式

2. Energy Storage With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的儲能計算

  • For two coils, total energy is:
    [
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    ]

  • The sign depends on whether the mutual inductance is aiding (+) or opposing (−)

  • 兩線圈儲能為：
    [
    W = \frac{1}{2} L_1 I_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} L_2 I_2^2 \pm M I_1 I_2
    ]

  • 其中符號由互感是互助（正號）或互消（負號）決定

3. Mutual Inductance Calculation 互感計算

  • Mutual inductance can be calculated using:
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • where (K) is the coupling coefficient

  • 互感計算公式：
    [
    M = K \sqrt{L_1 L_2}
    ]

  • 其中 (K) 是耦合係數

4. Series Aiding Inductors 串聯互助電感

  • For series aiding inductors:

    1. Calculate total inductance:
      [
      L_{\text{total}} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M
      ]
    2. Then calculate energy:
      [
      W = \frac{1}{2} L_{\text{total}} I^2
      ]

  • 串聯互助電感總儲能：

    1. 先計算總電感：
      [
      L_{\text{total}} = L_1 + L_2 + 2M
      ]
    2. 再用公式計算儲能：
      [
      W = \frac{1}{2} L_{\text{total}} I^2
      ]

Introduction to the Structure and Function of Inductors - 電感器的結構與功能介紹

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article provides a comprehensive overview of inductors, their structure, types, and working principles. It explains how inductors store energy and their role in electrical circuits.

本文全面介紹電感器的結構、種類和工作原理。解釋電感器如何儲存能量及其在電路中的作用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Basic Definition 基本定義

  • Symbol and Unit:

    • Symbol: L
    • Unit: Henry (H)
    • Named after Joseph Henry, who discovered inductance

  • 符號與單位：

    • 符號：L
    • 單位：亨利（H）
    • 紀念發現電感特性的科學家約瑟夫·亨利

2. Structure 結構

  • Core Components:

    • Conductor wire wound around a core
    • Core materials: air, iron, or magnetic material
    • Core material affects inductance value

  • 核心組件：

    • 導線繞在心材上
    • 心材可為空氣、鐵心或磁心
    • 心材影響電感量大小

3. Function 功能

  • Main Functions:

    • Converts electrical energy to magnetic energy
    • Stores energy in magnetic field
    • Stabilizes current flow
    • Prevents sudden current changes
    • Reduces magnetic interference

  • 主要功能：

    • 將電能轉換成磁能
    • 在磁場中儲存能量
    • 穩定電流
    • 防止電流瞬間變化
    • 減少磁干擾

4. Types 種類

  • Classification by Core Material:

    • Air-core inductors
    • Iron-core inductors
    • Magnetic-core inductors
    • Printed circuit inductors

  • 依心材分類：

    • 空氣心電感器
    • 鐵心電感器
    • 磁心電感器
    • 印刷電路電感器

5. Working Principle 工作原理

  • Charging Process:

    • Current gradually increases
    • Magnetic energy stored in inductor
    • Magnetic field builds up

  • 充電過程：

    • 電流逐漸增加
    • 磁能儲存在電感器中
    • 磁場逐漸建立

6. Discharging Process 放電過程

  • Characteristics:

    • Magnetic energy converts to electrical energy
    • Current gradually decreases
    • Connected lamp brightness changes accordingly

  • 特性：

    • 磁能轉換成電能
    • 電流逐漸減小
    • 連接的燈泡亮度隨之變化

7. Important Considerations 重要注意事項

  • Switch Operation:

    • Switches must be toggled simultaneously
    • Prevents circuit open-circuiting
    • Avoids magnetic energy loss

  • 開關操作：

    • 開關必須同時切換
    • 防止電路瞬間開路
    • 避免磁能損失

Basic Introduction to Magnetic Hysteresis Loop and Hysteresis Effect - 磁滯迴線與磁滯效應基礎介紹

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explores the fundamental concepts of magnetic hysteresis, its effects, and practical applications. Understanding hysteresis is crucial for various technologies, from data storage to electrical control systems.

本文探討磁滯的基本概念、效應及實際應用。理解磁滯現象對於從數據儲存到電氣控制系統等各種技術都至關重要。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Security Concerns 安全考量

  • Credit Card Security:

    • Magnetic stripes contain sensitive information
    • Vulnerable to copying through hysteresis effect
    • Potential risk of unauthorized duplication

  • 信用卡安全：

    • 磁條含有敏感資訊
    • 可能被磁滯效應複製
    • 存在未經授權複製的風險

2. Hysteresis Effect 磁滯效應

  • Basic Principle:

    • Ferromagnetic materials retain magnetization
    • Remanence persists after field removal
    • Key characteristic of magnetic materials

  • 基本原理：

    • 鐵磁性物質保留磁化狀態
    • 移除磁場後仍保有剩磁
    • 磁性物質的重要特性

3. Hysteresis Loop 磁滯迴線

  • Characteristics:

    • Illustrates magnetization cycle
    • Shows relationship between B and H
    • Demonstrates material’s magnetic behavior

  • 特性：

    • 描述磁化循環過程
    • 顯示B與H的關係
    • 展示材料的磁性行為

4. Key Properties 關鍵特性

  • Remanence and Coercivity:

    • Remanence: residual magnetism (B≠0 when H=0)
    • Coercivity: reverse field needed to demagnetize
    • Important for material selection

  • 剩磁與矯頑磁力：

    • 剩磁：殘留磁性（H=0時B≠0）
    • 矯頑磁力：退磁所需的反向磁場
    • 對材料選擇很重要

5. Energy Loss 能量損失

  • Hysteresis Loss:

    • Energy dissipated during magnetization cycles
    • Converts to heat
    • Affects efficiency of magnetic devices

  • 磁滯損失：

    • 磁化循環中的能量損耗
    • 轉換為熱能
    • 影響磁性設備的效率

6. Practical Applications 實際應用

  • Data Storage:

    • Audio tapes
    • Magnetic disks
    • Digital storage media

  • Control Systems:

    • Air conditioner control
    • Street light switching
    • Prevents frequent switching

  • 數據儲存：

    • 錄音帶
    • 磁碟片
    • 數位儲存媒體

  • 控制系統：

    • 冷氣機控制
    • 路燈開關
    • 避免頻繁切換

High School Basic Electricity: Parallel Inductance Calculation Examples - 高中基本電學：並聯總電感的運算範例

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in parallel circuits, both with and without considering mutual inductance effects. It includes practical examples and formulas for different scenarios.

本文解釋並聯電路中總電感的計算方法，包括考慮和不考慮互感效應的情況。內容包含實際範例和不同情況下的計算公式。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Parallel Inductance Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的並聯電感計算

  • The total inductance formula is:
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2} $$

  • Example: L1=20mH, L2=30mH, total inductance is 12mH

  • This is the basic formula for parallel inductors without mutual inductance

  • 總電感公式為：
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2} $$

  • 例：L1=20mH，L2=30mH，計算結果為12mH

  • 這是不考慮互感時並聯電感的基本公式

2. Parallel Inductance With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的並聯電感計算

  • The total inductance formula becomes:
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2} $$

  • Example: L1=8H, L2=2H, mutual inductance M=2H, total inductance is 2H

  • This formula accounts for the mutual inductance effect

  • 總電感公式變為：
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2} $$

  • 例：L1=8H，L2=2H，互感M=2H，計算結果為2H

  • 此公式考慮了互感效應

3. Mutual Inductance Direction Effects 互感方向影響

  • Same dot direction (head-to-head and tail-to-tail) indicates mutual assistance

  • This configuration affects the total inductance calculation

  • The mutual inductance term (M) can be positive or negative depending on the configuration

  • 打點方向相同（頭接頭尾接尾）屬於互助並聯

  • 這種配置會影響總電感的計算

  • 互感項（M）可為正值或負值，取決於配置方式

Parallel Combination of Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 電感器並聯組合(無互感)、(有互感)

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in parallel inductor combinations, covering both cases with and without mutual inductance. It includes formulas and practical considerations for different scenarios.

本文解釋電感器並聯組合的計算方法，包括有互感與無互感的情況。內容包含不同情況下的計算公式和實際考量。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Types of Parallel Combinations 並聯組合類型

  • Parallel inductors can be combined in two cases:

    • Without mutual inductance
    • With mutual inductance

  • Each case requires different calculation methods

  • 電感器並聯組合分為兩種情況：

    • 無互感
    • 有互感

  • 每種情況需要不同的計算方法

2. Parallel Inductors Without Mutual Inductance 無互感並聯電感

  • The formula is similar to parallel resistors:
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}
    $$

  • This is the basic formula for parallel inductors without mutual effects

  • 計算公式與電阻並聯相似：
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}
    $$

  • 這是不考慮互感時並聯電感的基本公式

3. Parallel Inductors With Mutual Inductance 有互感並聯電感

  • Need to determine if mutual effect is:

    • Assisting (same magnetic field direction)
    • Opposing (opposite magnetic field direction)

  • The formula becomes:
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 \mp 2M}
    $$

  • Sign convention:

    • Minus sign (-2M) for assisting mutual inductance
    • Plus sign (+2M) for opposing mutual inductance

  • 需判斷互感是：

    • 互助（磁場方向相同）
    • 互消（磁場方向相反）

  • 計算公式變為：
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 \mp 2M}
    $$

  • 符號規則：

    • 互助取負號（-2M），電感量較大
    • 互消取正號（+2M），電感量較小

Permeability and Magnetization Curve - 導磁係數與磁化曲線

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental concepts of magnetic permeability and magnetization curves, including their mathematical relationships and practical applications in different materials.

本文解釋磁導率與磁化曲線的基本概念，包括其數學關係及在不同材料中的實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Magnetic Permeability 導磁係數

  • Definition:

    • Symbol: μ (mu)
    • Formula: ( \mu = \mu_0 \times \mu_r )
    • Represents material’s ability to conduct magnetic flux

  • 定義：

    • 符號：μ
    • 公式：( \mu = \mu_0 \times \mu_r )
    • 表示物質導磁能力

2. Permeability Constants 導磁常數

  • Vacuum Permeability (μ₀):

    • Value: (4\pi \times 10^{-7})
    • Base reference for magnetic permeability
    • Universal constant

  • Relative Permeability (μr):

    • Ratio of material’s permeability to vacuum
    • Determines material’s magnetic properties
    • Dimensionless quantity

  • 真空導磁係數 (μ₀)：

    • 數值：(4\pi \times 10^{-7})
    • 磁導率的基準參考值
    • 通用常數

  • 相對導磁係數 (μr)：

    • 材料導磁率與真空的比值
    • 決定材料的磁性特性
    • 無因次量

3. Material Classification 材料分類

  • Ferromagnetic Materials:

    • μr ≫ 1
    • Examples: iron, silicon steel, steel, cobalt, nickel
    • Strong magnetic properties

  • Non-magnetic Materials:

    • μr = 1
    • Examples: vacuum
    • Linear B-H curve, no saturation

  • Paramagnetic Materials:

    • μr > 1
    • Examples: aluminum, platinum, tungsten
    • Magnetic flux aligns with field

  • Diamagnetic Materials:

    • μr < 1
    • Examples: gold, silver, copper
    • Magnetic flux opposes field

  • 強磁性材料：

    • μr ≫ 1
    • 例如：鐵、矽鋼、鋼、鈷、鎳
    • 強磁性特性

  • 非磁性材料：

    • μr = 1
    • 例如：真空
    • B-H曲線為直線，無飽和

  • 順磁性材料：

    • μr > 1
    • 例如：鋁、鉑、鎢
    • 磁通與磁場同向

  • 反磁性材料：

    • μr < 1
    • 例如：金、銀、銅
    • 磁通與磁場反向

4. Magnetization Process 磁化過程

  • Core Magnetization:

    • Current application magnetizes iron core
    • Magnetization strength increases with current
    • Magnetic domains align

  • 鐵心磁化：

    • 通電使鐵心磁化
    • 磁化強度隨電流增加
    • 磁域排列

5. B-H Curve Characteristics B-H曲線特性

  • Non-magnetic Materials:

    • Linear relationship
    • No saturation point
    • Direct proportionality

  • Magnetic Materials:

    • Non-linear relationship
    • Shows saturation point
    • Magnetic domain alignment affects flux

  • 非磁性材料：

    • 線性關係
    • 無飽和點
    • 直接比例關係

  • 磁性材料：

    • 非線性關係
    • 顯示飽和點
    • 磁域排列影響磁通

6. Magnetic Saturation 磁飽和

  • Definition:

    • Complete alignment of magnetic domains
    • Further H increase doesn’t significantly increase B
    • Maximum magnetization state

  • 定義：

    • 磁域完全排列
    • 增加H不會顯著增加B
    • 最大磁化狀態

Detailed Explanation of Self-Inductance Definition - 自感量定義詳解

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article provides a detailed explanation of self-inductance, its definition, formula, and practical significance in electromagnetic systems. It helps readers understand the fundamental concept of how coils generate magnetic flux in response to current changes.

本文詳細解釋自感量的定義、公式及其在電磁系統中的實際意義。幫助讀者理解線圈如何對電流變化產生磁通量的基本概念。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Definition of Self-Inductance 自感量定義

  • Self-inductance (L) is the ratio of the magnetic flux linkage to the current in a single coil

  • It represents the coil’s ability to generate magnetic flux in response to current

  • **自感量（L）**是指單一線圈中，當電流通過時，該線圈自身產生的磁通鏈與電流的比值

  • 表示線圈對電流產生磁通量的能力

2. Mathematical Formula 數學公式

  • Formula:
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]
    where:

    • N: Number of turns in the coil
    • Φ: Magnetic flux (in Weber, Wb)
    • I: Current through the coil (in Ampere, A)

  • 公式：
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]
    其中：

    • N：線圈的匝數
    • Φ：磁通量（單位韋柏 Wb）
    • I：通過線圈的電流（單位安培 A）

3. Physical Meaning 物理意義

  • When current I flows through the coil, it generates magnetic flux Φ

  • The product of flux and number of turns (NΦ) gives the flux linkage

  • The ratio of flux linkage to current is the self-inductance

  • 當電流 I 流過線圈時，會在該線圈中產生磁通量 Φ

  • 磁通量與匝數的乘積（NΦ）得到磁通鏈

  • 磁通鏈與電流的比值就是自感量

4. Significance 重要性

  • Higher self-inductance means:

    • Stronger response to current changes
    • Small current changes can produce large flux linkage
    • Greater ability to generate magnetic flux

  • 自感量越大表示：

    • 對電流變化的反應越強
    • 少量電流變化就能產生較大的磁通鏈
    • 產生磁通的能力越強

5. Practical Understanding 實際理解

  • Self-inductance is a measure of a coil’s ability to generate magnetic flux

  • It is a fundamental parameter in electromagnetic systems

  • It affects how the coil responds to changing currents

  • 自感量是衡量線圈「自我產生磁通能力」的參數

  • 是電磁系統中的基本參數

  • 影響線圈對變化電流的響應方式

Self-Inductance and Mutual Inductance - 自感與互感

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental concepts of self-inductance and mutual inductance in electromagnetic systems. It covers the basic principles, mathematical relationships, and practical applications of these important electromagnetic phenomena.

本文解釋電磁系統中自感和互感的基本概念。內容涵蓋這些重要電磁現象的基本原理、數學關係和實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Self-Inductance 自感

  • Definition:

    • The magnetic flux generated by current flowing through a single coil
    • Related to the coil’s own current
    • A fundamental property of any coil

  • 定義：

    • 單一線圈通電時產生的磁通量
    • 與該線圈自身的電流相關
    • 任何線圈的基本特性

2. Mutual Inductance 互感

  • Definition:

    • Occurs when two coils are in proximity
    • Current in one coil produces magnetic flux that links to the other coil
    • Denoted by capital M

  • 定義：

    • 當兩個相鄰線圈存在時發生
    • 一個線圈的電流會產生磁通量，並交鏈到另一個線圈
    • 表示為大寫M

3. Magnetic Flux Components 磁通量分解

  • Flux in coil 1:

    • Total flux Φ1 splits into:
      • Φ11: Leakage flux (not linking the other coil)
      • Φ12: Mutual flux (linking the other coil)

  • 線圈1的磁通：

    • 總磁通Φ1分為：
      • Φ11：漏磁通（未交鏈到另一線圈）
      • Φ12：交鏈磁通（穿過另一線圈）

4. Self-Inductance Formula 自感量公式

  • Formula for coil 1:
    [
    L_1 = \frac{N_1 \Phi_1}{I_1}
    ]
    where:

    • N1: Number of turns
    • I1: Current

  • 線圈1的公式：
    [
    L_1 = \frac{N_1 \Phi_1}{I_1}
    ]
    其中：

    • N1：線圈匝數
    • I1：電流

5. Mutual Inductance Formula 互感量公式

  • Formula for mutual inductance:
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}
    ]
    This represents the mutual inductance from coil 1 to coil 2

  • 互感量公式：
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}
    ]
    表示線圈1對線圈2的互感

6. Coil 2 Relationships 線圈2的關係

  • Similar definitions apply to coil 2:

    • Self-inductance L2
    • Mutual inductance M21

  • 線圈2也有類似的定義：

    • 自感量L2
    • 互感量M21
