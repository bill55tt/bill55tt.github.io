High School Basic Electricity: Parallel Inductance Calculation Examples - 高中基本電學：並聯總電感的運算範例

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in parallel circuits, both with and without considering mutual inductance effects. It includes practical examples and formulas for different scenarios.

本文解釋並聯電路中總電感的計算方法，包括考慮和不考慮互感效應的情況。內容包含實際範例和不同情況下的計算公式。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Parallel Inductance Without Mutual Inductance 不考慮互感的並聯電感計算

  • The total inductance formula is:
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2} $$

  • Example: L1=20mH, L2=30mH, total inductance is 12mH

  • This is the basic formula for parallel inductors without mutual inductance

  • 總電感公式為：
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2} $$

  • 例：L1=20mH，L2=30mH，計算結果為12mH

  • 這是不考慮互感時並聯電感的基本公式

2. Parallel Inductance With Mutual Inductance 考慮互感的並聯電感計算

  • The total inductance formula becomes:
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2} $$

  • Example: L1=8H, L2=2H, mutual inductance M=2H, total inductance is 2H

  • This formula accounts for the mutual inductance effect

  • 總電感公式變為：
    $$ L_{total} = \frac{L_1 + L_2 - 2M}{L_1 \times L_2 - M^2} $$

  • 例：L1=8H，L2=2H，互感M=2H，計算結果為2H

  • 此公式考慮了互感效應

3. Mutual Inductance Direction Effects 互感方向影響

  • Same dot direction (head-to-head and tail-to-tail) indicates mutual assistance

  • This configuration affects the total inductance calculation

  • The mutual inductance term (M) can be positive or negative depending on the configuration

  • 打點方向相同（頭接頭尾接尾）屬於互助並聯

  • 這種配置會影響總電感的計算

  • 互感項（M）可為正值或負值，取決於配置方式

Parallel Combination of Inductors: With and Without Mutual Inductance - 電感器並聯組合(無互感)、(有互感)

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the methods for calculating total inductance in parallel inductor combinations, covering both cases with and without mutual inductance. It includes formulas and practical considerations for different scenarios.

本文解釋電感器並聯組合的計算方法，包括有互感與無互感的情況。內容包含不同情況下的計算公式和實際考量。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Types of Parallel Combinations 並聯組合類型

  • Parallel inductors can be combined in two cases:

    • Without mutual inductance
    • With mutual inductance

  • Each case requires different calculation methods

  • 電感器並聯組合分為兩種情況：

    • 無互感
    • 有互感

  • 每種情況需要不同的計算方法

2. Parallel Inductors Without Mutual Inductance 無互感並聯電感

  • The formula is similar to parallel resistors:
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}
    $$

  • This is the basic formula for parallel inductors without mutual effects

  • 計算公式與電阻並聯相似：
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 \times L_2}{L_1 + L_2}
    $$

  • 這是不考慮互感時並聯電感的基本公式

3. Parallel Inductors With Mutual Inductance 有互感並聯電感

  • Need to determine if mutual effect is:

    • Assisting (same magnetic field direction)
    • Opposing (opposite magnetic field direction)

  • The formula becomes:
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 \mp 2M}
    $$

  • Sign convention:

    • Minus sign (-2M) for assisting mutual inductance
    • Plus sign (+2M) for opposing mutual inductance

  • 需判斷互感是：

    • 互助（磁場方向相同）
    • 互消（磁場方向相反）

  • 計算公式變為：
    $$
    L_T = \frac{L_1 L_2 - M^2}{L_1 + L_2 \mp 2M}
    $$

  • 符號規則：

    • 互助取負號（-2M），電感量較大
    • 互消取正號（+2M），電感量較小

Permeability and Magnetization Curve - 導磁係數與磁化曲線

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental concepts of magnetic permeability and magnetization curves, including their mathematical relationships and practical applications in different materials.

本文解釋磁導率與磁化曲線的基本概念，包括其數學關係及在不同材料中的實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Magnetic Permeability 導磁係數

  • Definition:

    • Symbol: μ (mu)
    • Formula: ( \mu = \mu_0 \times \mu_r )
    • Represents material’s ability to conduct magnetic flux

  • 定義：

    • 符號：μ
    • 公式：( \mu = \mu_0 \times \mu_r )
    • 表示物質導磁能力

2. Permeability Constants 導磁常數

  • Vacuum Permeability (μ₀):

    • Value: (4\pi \times 10^{-7})
    • Base reference for magnetic permeability
    • Universal constant

  • Relative Permeability (μr):

    • Ratio of material’s permeability to vacuum
    • Determines material’s magnetic properties
    • Dimensionless quantity

  • 真空導磁係數 (μ₀)：

    • 數值：(4\pi \times 10^{-7})
    • 磁導率的基準參考值
    • 通用常數

  • 相對導磁係數 (μr)：

    • 材料導磁率與真空的比值
    • 決定材料的磁性特性
    • 無因次量

3. Material Classification 材料分類

  • Ferromagnetic Materials:

    • μr ≫ 1
    • Examples: iron, silicon steel, steel, cobalt, nickel
    • Strong magnetic properties

  • Non-magnetic Materials:

    • μr = 1
    • Examples: vacuum
    • Linear B-H curve, no saturation

  • Paramagnetic Materials:

    • μr > 1
    • Examples: aluminum, platinum, tungsten
    • Magnetic flux aligns with field

  • Diamagnetic Materials:

    • μr < 1
    • Examples: gold, silver, copper
    • Magnetic flux opposes field

  • 強磁性材料：

    • μr ≫ 1
    • 例如：鐵、矽鋼、鋼、鈷、鎳
    • 強磁性特性

  • 非磁性材料：

    • μr = 1
    • 例如：真空
    • B-H曲線為直線，無飽和

  • 順磁性材料：

    • μr > 1
    • 例如：鋁、鉑、鎢
    • 磁通與磁場同向

  • 反磁性材料：

    • μr < 1
    • 例如：金、銀、銅
    • 磁通與磁場反向

4. Magnetization Process 磁化過程

  • Core Magnetization:

    • Current application magnetizes iron core
    • Magnetization strength increases with current
    • Magnetic domains align

  • 鐵心磁化：

    • 通電使鐵心磁化
    • 磁化強度隨電流增加
    • 磁域排列

5. B-H Curve Characteristics B-H曲線特性

  • Non-magnetic Materials:

    • Linear relationship
    • No saturation point
    • Direct proportionality

  • Magnetic Materials:

    • Non-linear relationship
    • Shows saturation point
    • Magnetic domain alignment affects flux

  • 非磁性材料：

    • 線性關係
    • 無飽和點
    • 直接比例關係

  • 磁性材料：

    • 非線性關係
    • 顯示飽和點
    • 磁域排列影響磁通

6. Magnetic Saturation 磁飽和

  • Definition:

    • Complete alignment of magnetic domains
    • Further H increase doesn’t significantly increase B
    • Maximum magnetization state

  • 定義：

    • 磁域完全排列
    • 增加H不會顯著增加B
    • 最大磁化狀態

Detailed Explanation of Self-Inductance Definition - 自感量定義詳解

Posted on 2025-05-14 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article provides a detailed explanation of self-inductance, its definition, formula, and practical significance in electromagnetic systems. It helps readers understand the fundamental concept of how coils generate magnetic flux in response to current changes.

本文詳細解釋自感量的定義、公式及其在電磁系統中的實際意義。幫助讀者理解線圈如何對電流變化產生磁通量的基本概念。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Definition of Self-Inductance 自感量定義

  • Self-inductance (L) is the ratio of the magnetic flux linkage to the current in a single coil

  • It represents the coil’s ability to generate magnetic flux in response to current

  • **自感量（L）**是指單一線圈中，當電流通過時，該線圈自身產生的磁通鏈與電流的比值

  • 表示線圈對電流產生磁通量的能力

2. Mathematical Formula 數學公式

  • Formula:
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]
    where:

    • N: Number of turns in the coil
    • Φ: Magnetic flux (in Weber, Wb)
    • I: Current through the coil (in Ampere, A)

  • 公式：
    [
    L = \frac{N \Phi}{I}
    ]
    其中：

    • N：線圈的匝數
    • Φ：磁通量（單位韋柏 Wb）
    • I：通過線圈的電流（單位安培 A）

3. Physical Meaning 物理意義

  • When current I flows through the coil, it generates magnetic flux Φ

  • The product of flux and number of turns (NΦ) gives the flux linkage

  • The ratio of flux linkage to current is the self-inductance

  • 當電流 I 流過線圈時，會在該線圈中產生磁通量 Φ

  • 磁通量與匝數的乘積（NΦ）得到磁通鏈

  • 磁通鏈與電流的比值就是自感量

4. Significance 重要性

  • Higher self-inductance means:

    • Stronger response to current changes
    • Small current changes can produce large flux linkage
    • Greater ability to generate magnetic flux

  • 自感量越大表示：

    • 對電流變化的反應越強
    • 少量電流變化就能產生較大的磁通鏈
    • 產生磁通的能力越強

5. Practical Understanding 實際理解

  • Self-inductance is a measure of a coil’s ability to generate magnetic flux

  • It is a fundamental parameter in electromagnetic systems

  • It affects how the coil responds to changing currents

  • 自感量是衡量線圈「自我產生磁通能力」的參數

  • 是電磁系統中的基本參數

  • 影響線圈對變化電流的響應方式

Self and Mutual Inductance: Concepts and Calculations - 自感與互感：原理與計算

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explains the fundamental concepts of self-inductance and mutual inductance in electromagnetic systems. It covers the basic principles, mathematical relationships, and practical applications of these important electromagnetic phenomena.

本文解釋電磁系統中自感和互感的基本概念。內容涵蓋這些重要電磁現象的基本原理、數學關係和實際應用。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Self-Inductance 自感

  • Definition:

    • The magnetic flux generated by current flowing through a single coil
    • Related to the coil’s own current
    • A fundamental property of any coil

  • 定義：

    • 單一線圈通電時產生的磁通量
    • 與該線圈自身的電流相關
    • 任何線圈的基本特性

2. Mutual Inductance 互感

  • Definition:

    • Occurs when two coils are in proximity
    • Current in one coil produces magnetic flux that links to the other coil
    • Denoted by capital M

  • 定義：

    • 當兩個相鄰線圈存在時發生
    • 一個線圈的電流會產生磁通量，並交鏈到另一個線圈
    • 表示為大寫M

3. Magnetic Flux Components 磁通量分解

  • Flux in coil 1:

    • Total flux Φ1 splits into:
      • Φ11: Leakage flux (not linking the other coil)
      • Φ12: Mutual flux (linking the other coil)

  • 線圈1的磁通：

    • 總磁通Φ1分為：
      • Φ11：漏磁通（未交鏈到另一線圈）
      • Φ12：交鏈磁通（穿過另一線圈）

4. Self-Inductance Formula 自感量公式

  • Formula for coil 1:
    [
    L_1 = \frac{N_1 \Phi_1}{I_1}
    ]
    where:

    • N1: Number of turns
    • I1: Current

  • 線圈1的公式：
    [
    L_1 = \frac{N_1 \Phi_1}{I_1}
    ]
    其中：

    • N1：線圈匝數
    • I1：電流

5. Mutual Inductance Formula 互感量公式

  • Formula for mutual inductance:
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}
    ]
    This represents the mutual inductance from coil 1 to coil 2

  • 互感量公式：
    [
    M_{12} = \frac{N_2 \Phi_{12}}{I_1}
    ]
    表示線圈1對線圈2的互感

6. Coil 2 Relationships 線圈2的關係

  • Similar definitions apply to coil 2:

    • Self-inductance L2
    • Mutual inductance M21

  • 線圈2也有類似的定義：

    • 自感量L2
    • 互感量M21
