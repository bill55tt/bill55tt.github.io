High School Basic Electricity: Parallel Circuits - 高中基本電學：並聯電路

Posted on 2025-05-05 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article explores the fundamental concepts of parallel circuits, including their definition, characteristics, and mathematical relationships. Understanding parallel circuits is essential for analyzing complex electrical systems and designing efficient circuits.

引言

本文探討並聯電路的基本概念，包括其定義、特性以及數學關係。理解並聯電路對於分析複雜電氣系統和設計高效電路至關重要。

Key Points

Parallel Circuit Definition and Characteristics

  1. Definition:
    A parallel circuit consists of two or more components, where one end of each component is connected together and the other end is also connected together.
  2. Characteristics:
    Equal Voltage: The voltage across all components is the same.
    Independence: If one component fails, it does not affect the operation of other components.
    Total Resistance: The formula for total resistance ( R_T ) is:
    $$\frac{1}{R_T} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{R_n}$$
    Conductance: The total conductance ( G_T ) is the sum of the conductances of each branch.

重點歸納

並聯電路定義及特性

  1. 定義：
    並聯電路由兩個或以上元件組成，所有元件的一端相連，另一端也相連。
  2. 特性：
    電壓相等：所有元件的電壓相同。
    獨立性：某一元件斷路不影響其他元件的運作。
    總電阻：總電阻 ( R_T ) 計算公式為：
    $$\frac{1}{R_T} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{R_n}$$
    電導：總電導 ( G_T ) 為各支路電導之和。

High School Basic Electricity: Fundamental Theories of Electricity - Power Part A - 高中基本電學：電學基礎理論 - 電功率 PART A

Posted on 2025-05-05 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Title & Introduction Outline

Title: High School Basic Electricity: Basic Theory of Electricity - Power Part A: Power, Horsepower, Electric Power, Examples

  • Power
  • Horsepower
  • Electric Power
  • Examples

Key Points

  1. Power
    Definition: The work done or energy used per unit time.
    Formula: $P = \frac{W}{t}$
    Unit: Watt (W)
  2. Horsepower
    Definition: The power required to lift a 76 kg weight by 1 meter in 1 second.
    Unit: Horsepower (hp)
    Conversion: 1 hp = 746 W
  3. Electric Power
    Definition: The product of voltage and current in a circuit.
    Formula: $P = VI$
    Unit: Watt (W)
  4. Examples
    • Example 1: A lamp has a potential difference of 12 V and a current of 5 A. What is the power consumed?
      $P = VI = 12 \times 5 = 60\,W$
    • Example 2: A prime mover lifts a 1000 N weight by 20 m in 4 seconds. What is the output power and its horsepower?
      Work $W = FS = 1000 \times 20 = 20,000\,J$
      Power $P = \frac{W}{t} = \frac{20,000}{4} = 5,000\,W = 5\,kW$
      Horsepower $= \frac{5,000}{746} \approx 6.7\,hp$

Vocabulary

  • Power
  • Horsepower
  • Electric Power
  • Watt

標題與前言大綱

標題：高中基本電學_電學基礎理論_電功率_PART A 功率、馬力、電功率、範例

  • 功率
  • 馬力
  • 電功率
  • 範例

詳細重點統整

  1. 功率
    定義：單位時間內所做的功或能量。
    公式：$P = \frac{W}{t}$
    單位：瓦特 (Watt, W)
  2. 馬力
    定義：一匹馬在1秒鐘內將76公斤的重物提高1公尺。
    單位：馬力 (Horsepower, hp)
    換算：1 hp = 746 W
  3. 電功率
    定義：電路中電壓和電流的乘積。
    公式：$P = VI$
    單位：瓦特 (Watt, W)
  4. 範例
    • 範例一：電燈兩端的電位差12伏特，流過電燈的電流5安培，消耗功率多少瓦特？
      $P = VI = 12 \times 5 = 60\,W$
    • 範例二：有一個原動機在4秒鐘將1000牛頓的重物提高20公尺，輸出功率多少瓦還有它的馬力是多少？
      做功 $W = FS = 1000 \times 20 = 20,000\,J$
      功率 $P = \frac{W}{t} = \frac{20,000}{4} = 5,000\,W = 5\,kW$
      馬力 $= \frac{5,000}{746} \approx 6.7\,hp$

英文翻譯

  • 功率: Power
  • 馬力: Horsepower
  • 電功率: Electric Power
  • 瓦特: Watt

High School Basic Electricity: Fundamental Theories of Electricity - Power Part B - 高中基本電學：電學基礎理論 - 電功率 PART B

Posted on 2025-05-05 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This lesson, taught by Mr. Chen Zhengxu, introduces the concepts of electric energy and kilowatt-hour (kWh), including practical calculation examples.

課程簡介

高中基本電學_電學基礎理論_電功率_PART B 電能與電度，由陳政旭老師主講，主要介紹電能與電度的概念。

Key Points

  • Meaning of Electric Energy: Electric energy is the energy consumed by an electrical appliance with power P (watts) over t seconds.
    Formula: $W = P \cdot t$, unit: joule (J).
  • Kilowatt-Hour (kWh): kWh is the unit used by power companies to measure electric energy. 1 kWh = the energy consumed by a 1000-watt appliance used for 1 hour.
  • Conversion between Electric Energy and kWh: 1 kWh = $3.6 \times 10^6$ joules.

Examples

  • Example 1: How much energy (in joules) does a 4000-watt motor consume in 30 minutes?
    $W = 4000 \cdot (30 \cdot 60) = 7.2 \times 10^6$ J
  • Example 2: Calculating a household's daily electricity cost.
    Total usage: 6 kWh.
    Total cost: $6 \cdot 3 = 18$ NTD/day.
    30-day total: $18 \cdot 30 = 540$ NTD.

Vocabulary

  • Electric Energy
  • Electricity Degree or Kilowatt-Hour (kWh)
  • Electric Power

影片重點統整

  • 電能的涵義：電能是功率為P瓦特的電器用品，使用t秒鐘所消耗的能量。
    公式：$W = P \cdot t$，單位為焦耳。
  • 電度：電度是電力公司對電能的計算方式。1度電 = 1000瓦的功率的電器用品，使用1個小時所消耗的電能。
  • 電能與電度的換算：1度電 = $3.6 \times 10^6$焦耳。

範例

  • 範例一：4000瓦的電動機運轉30分鐘，共消耗電能多少焦耳？
    $W = 4000 \cdot (30 \cdot 60) = 7.2 \times 10^6$焦耳。
  • 範例二：某家庭每日用電量計算，總共要付多少電費？
    總用電量：6度電。
    總電費：$6 \cdot 3 = 18$元/天。
    30天總電費：$18 \cdot 30 = 540$元。

英文翻譯

  • 電能：Electric Energy
  • 電度：Electricity Degree or Kilowatt-Hour (kWh)
  • 電功率：Electric Power

High School Basic Electricity: Fundamental Theories of Electricity - Power Part C - 高中基本電學：電學基礎理論 - 電功率 PART C

Posted on 2025-05-05 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This lesson covers the concept of efficiency in electrical power, including its definition, formula, and practical examples. Understanding efficiency is crucial for analyzing energy conversion systems and optimizing performance.

Key Points

  • Efficiency Definition: Efficiency is defined as the ratio of output energy to input energy, expressed as a percentage.
  • Efficiency Formula: Efficiency = (Output Energy / Input Energy) × 100%
  • Energy Conversion System: An energy conversion system can consist of multiple subsystems connected in series, with the overall efficiency being the product of the efficiencies of the individual subsystems.

Example Analysis

  • Example 1: A water pump motor with an input power of 880 watts and an efficiency of 0.85, find output power and loss power.
  • Example 2: A power system consisting of two subsystems with efficiencies of 0.8 and 0.9, find the overall efficiency and output energy.

前言

本課程介紹電功率中的效率概念，包括定義、公式與實際範例。理解效率對於分析能量轉換系統與提升效能至關重要。

詳細重點統整

  • 效率的定義：效率是指輸出能量與輸入能量的比值，以百分率表示。
  • 效率公式：效率 = (輸出能量 / 輸入能量) × 100%
  • 能量轉換系統：能量轉換系統可以由多個子系統串接而成，總效率為各子系統效率的乘積。

範例解析

  • 範例一：抽水馬達輸入功率 880 瓦，效率 0.85，求輸出功率和損失功率。
  • 範例二：電力系統由兩個子系統組成，效率分別為 0.8 和 0.9，求總效率和輸出能量。

High School Basic Electricity: Resistance and Resistivity Example - 高中基本電學：電阻與電阻係數範例

Posted on 2025-05-05 In Electronics , Physics , Basic Electronics Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article demonstrates a practical example of calculating the resistance of a copper wire, showing the step-by-step process of applying the resistance formula and unit conversions.

引言

本文展示了一個計算銅線電阻的實際範例，說明如何應用電阻公式和進行單位轉換的步驟。

Key Points

  1. Problem Background:
    Example: Calculate the resistance of a copper wire that is 2 kilometers long and has a cross-sectional area of 14 square millimeters.
  2. Parameter Identification:
    Length \( L = 2000 \, \text{m} \) (converted from kilometers)
    Cross-sectional area \( A = 14 \times 10^{-6} \, \text{m}^2 \) (converted from square millimeters)
    Resistivity of copper \( \rho = 1.724 \times 10^{-8} \, \Omega \cdot \text{m} \)
  3. Resistance Formula:
    Using the formula \( R = \frac{\rho L}{A} \)
    Calculated resistance \( R = 2.463 \, \Omega \)
  4. Unit Conversion:
    Ensure all parameters are in MKS units (meters, kilograms, seconds).

重點歸納

  1. 題目背景：
    例題：計算一條長2公里、截面積14平方公釐的銅線電阻。
  2. 參數標示：
    長度 \( L = 2000 \, \text{m} \)（轉換自公里）
    截面積 \( A = 14 \times 10^{-6} \, \text{m}^2 \)（轉換自平方公釐）
    銅的電阻係數 \( \rho = 1.724 \times 10^{-8} \, \Omega \cdot \text{m} \)
  3. 電阻公式：
    使用公式 \( R = \frac{\rho L}{A} \)
    計算得出電阻 \( R = 2.463 \, \Omega \)
  4. 單位轉換：
    確保所有參數使用MKS制（米、公斤、秒）。
