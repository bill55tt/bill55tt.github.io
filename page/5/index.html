Magnetic Field Intensity of a Long Straight Conductor - 長直導線的磁場強度

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article introduces the concept and calculation of the magnetic field intensity around a long straight conductor, drawing analogies from the electric field of a parallel plate capacitor. It covers the formula, unit, direction, and practical examples.

本文介紹長直導線周圍磁場強度的概念與計算，並以平行板電容器的電場強度作類比推導，涵蓋公式、單位、方向與實例。

  • [Show Both Languages 同時顯示]

Key Points 重點歸納

English

  • The concept of electric field intensity in a parallel plate capacitor is used to analogously derive the magnetic field intensity of a long straight conductor.
  • In a parallel plate capacitor, electric field intensity equals voltage divided by the distance between plates.
  • The magnetic field intensity around a long straight conductor depends on the current (magnetomotive force) and the length of the magnetic field line (magnetic path length).
  • Magnetic field intensity formula:
    $$
    H = \frac{I}{2 \pi R}
    $$
    where (I) is the current in amperes and (R) is the radius (distance from the wire) in meters.
  • The unit of magnetic field intensity is ampere-turn per meter (AT/m).
  • The direction of the magnetic field follows the right-hand rule, with magnetic field lines forming circles around the conductor.
  • Example: For a 10-ampere current at 10 cm from the conductor, the magnetic field intensity is (\frac{50}{\pi} , \text{AT/m}), directed into the plane.

中文

  • 利用平行板電容器的電場強度概念，類比推導長直導線的磁場強度。
  • 平行板電容器中，電場強度為電壓除以兩極板距離。
  • 長直導線產生的磁場強度與電流（磁動勢）及磁力線長度（磁路長）有關。
  • 磁場強度公式：
    $$
    H = \frac{I}{2 \pi R}
    $$
    其中 (I) 是電流（安培），(R) 是距離導線的半徑（公尺）。
  • 磁場強度單位為安匝每公尺（AT/m）。
  • 磁場方向依安培右手定則判斷，磁力線繞導線形成圓周。
  • 範例：通入10安培電流，距離導線10公分處磁場強度為 (\frac{50}{\pi} , \text{AT/m})，方向射入紙面。

Magnetic Flux Units and Calculations - 磁力線單位與計算

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-18 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction

This article explains the fundamental units of magnetic flux, their relationships, and practical calculations in both SI and CGS systems.

Key Points

  • Magnetic Flux Unit: Maxwell

    • Unit of magnetic lines of force
    • CGS unit of magnetic flux
    • Fundamental unit in electromagnetic calculations

  • Magnetic Flux (Φ, Phi)

    • Total number of magnetic lines of force
    • Measured in Weber (SI unit)
    • Conversion: 1 Weber = 10^8 Maxwell

  • Magnetic Flux Density (B)

    • Magnetic flux per unit area
    • Formula:
      $$
      B = \frac{\Phi}{A}
      $$
    • Units:
      • Tesla (SI)
      • Gauss (CGS)
      • Conversion: 1 Tesla = 10^4 Gauss

  • Unit Conversions

    • 1 Weber = 10^8 Maxwell
    • 1 Tesla = 10^4 Gauss
    • Essential for system conversion

  • Example Calculation

    • Area: 10 cm²
    • Flux: 10^6 Maxwell
    • Flux density: 10^5 Gauss
    • Converted to Tesla: 10 Tesla

Basic Introduction to Magnetic Hysteresis Loop and Hysteresis Effect - 磁滯迴線與磁滯效應基礎介紹

Posted on 2025-05-14 Edited on 2025-05-16 In Physics , Electromagnetism Views: Disqus:

Introduction 引言

This article explores the fundamental concepts of magnetic hysteresis, its effects, and practical applications. Understanding hysteresis is crucial for various technologies, from data storage to electrical control systems.

本文探討磁滯的基本概念、效應及實際應用。理解磁滯現象對於從數據儲存到電氣控制系統等各種技術都至關重要。

Key Points 重點歸納

1. Security Concerns 安全考量

  • Credit Card Security:

    • Magnetic stripes contain sensitive information
    • Vulnerable to copying through hysteresis effect
    • Potential risk of unauthorized duplication

  • 信用卡安全：

    • 磁條含有敏感資訊
    • 可能被磁滯效應複製
    • 存在未經授權複製的風險

2. Hysteresis Effect 磁滯效應

  • Basic Principle:

    • Ferromagnetic materials retain magnetization
    • Remanence persists after field removal
    • Key characteristic of magnetic materials

  • 基本原理：

    • 鐵磁性物質保留磁化狀態
    • 移除磁場後仍保有剩磁
    • 磁性物質的重要特性

3. Hysteresis Loop 磁滯迴線

  • Characteristics:

    • Illustrates magnetization cycle
    • Shows relationship between B and H
    • Demonstrates material’s magnetic behavior

  • 特性：

    • 描述磁化循環過程
    • 顯示B與H的關係
    • 展示材料的磁性行為

4. Key Properties 關鍵特性

  • Remanence and Coercivity:

    • Remanence: residual magnetism (B≠0 when H=0)
    • Coercivity: reverse field needed to demagnetize
    • Important for material selection

  • 剩磁與矯頑磁力：

    • 剩磁：殘留磁性（H=0時B≠0）
    • 矯頑磁力：退磁所需的反向磁場
    • 對材料選擇很重要

5. Energy Loss 能量損失

  • Hysteresis Loss:

    • Energy dissipated during magnetization cycles
    • Converts to heat
    • Affects efficiency of magnetic devices

  • 磁滯損失：

    • 磁化循環中的能量損耗
    • 轉換為熱能
    • 影響磁性設備的效率

6. Practical Applications 實際應用

  • Data Storage:

    • Audio tapes
    • Magnetic disks
    • Digital storage media

  • Control Systems:

    • Air conditioner control
    • Street light switching
    • Prevents frequent switching

  • 數據儲存：

    • 錄音帶
    • 磁碟片
    • 數位儲存媒體

  • 控制系統：

    • 冷氣機控制
    • 路燈開關
    • 避免頻繁切換
